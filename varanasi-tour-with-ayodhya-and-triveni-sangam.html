Itinerary At Glance:

In this 6N/7D package you come to visit various holy cities in & around Kashi. Starting with arriving in Varanasi - is older than history, older than tradition, older even than legend & looks twice as old as all of them put together, said the popular American writer after visiting Varanasi in the late 19th century. Varanasi is among the oldest continuously lived cities in the world.  You will also visit Gaya and BodhGaya Tour, Gaya finds mention in the great epics, Ramayana and Mahabharata. Lord Rama along with Sita and Lakshmana visited Gaya to offer PIND-DAAN to their father Dasharath. In Mahabharata, the place has been identified as Gayapuri. Later you will visit Bodhgaya- is a religious site and place of pilgrimage associated with the Lord Buddha who got enlightenment here. You will also visit Allahabad which is also known as Prayagraj, where you can take a bath in the Triveni Sangam package. You also visit the holy city of Chitrakoot and Ayodhya Tour. Ayodhya, also known as Saket, is an ancient city of India,which is the birthplace of Rama and the setting of the epic Ramayana.​ Ghum India Ghum is a leading travel company in Varanasi, tour operators for Varanasi, travel agents in Varanasi. 

Itinerary at Glance :-
Day 01:- Varanasi arrival and Local
On arrival at Oldest city of the world Varanasi meet and greet our representative at Varanasi International Airport or Varanasi Railway Station, Later do hotel check in and after a few hours of relaxation. This afternoon post lunch you will visit to Annapurna Devi, Manas Temple, Vishwanath Temple, Sankat Mochan, Durga Kund, Vishalakshi Devi and Birla Temple – Banaras Hindu University, later visit famous ghats of Varanasi:- Harishchandra, Manikarnakaa also popularly known as burning ghat (It is believed that a dead human's soul finds salvation, when cremated here, after spending some time here you will find yourself at completely different world), Dashashwamedh Ghat, May visit Nepali temple Varanasi it is build on similar pattern of Pashupati temple in Kathmandu etcetc. In the evening, Participate in the world famous Ganga Aarti at Dashashwamedh Ghat. After completion of places to visit in Varanasi or best itinerary for Varanasi tour, enjoy your overnight stay in Kashi post spiritual tour packages in india.

Day 02:- Varanasi (Morning boat ride)- Sarnath- Varanasi
Today's early morning, go for a sunrise boat ride on river Ganges also known as subah-e-banaras. This boat ride will take you to a different world. It gives you a chance to see the tradition and culture of ancient city Kashi. After this amazing experience, return back to your hotel to have your breakfast and Later in the afternoon, visit Sarnath which is 9kms from Varanasi,  After attaining enlightenment at Bodh Gaya, Sarnath is the place where Lord Buddha gave his first sermon or lessons on dharma to his first five important disciples. On arrival first visit sacred Dhamek Stupa -one of the most visited stupa in India and known as the most important Buddhist shrine in the world. Later also See Sarnath museum, Wat Thai Sarnath Temple, Main temple and Chaukhandi Stupa of Sarnath, Post tour return back to hotel and rest of the time free to explore the city by yourself. Overnight in Varanasi.

Day 03:- Varanasi to Gaya or Bodh Gaya to Varanasi (250kms/ 6-7hrs each way)
Wake up early this morning and get ready, Today at 6am we will drive you for Gaya day tour from Varanasi (ask hotel for Pack breakfast), With Arrival in Gaya on your Varanasi Gaya Allahabad tour packages, Gaya and Bodh Gaya are twin spiritual cities situated in Neighbouring state Bihar and it is believed that Lord Rama along with Sita and Lakshman came to Gaya for performing PIND-DAAN ritual to their father Dasharath, where one can do darshan of Vishnu padam in Gaya and also you may do puja or yajna for forefathers or departed souls of family (Shradh puja or Gaya pind daan puja). Later post lunch drive to Bodh Gaya which is UNESCO world heritage site and is the place where lord Buddha has done meditation for 6 years in Anandvan forest. In Bodh Gaya visit historical Mahabodhi temple which is main temple of Bodh Gaya and where Lord Buddha attained enlightenment under sacred Bodhi Tree, Also visit China temple, Thai Temple, Japanese Temple and Later Post completion of Places to visit tour in Bodh Gaya or best itinerary tour for Gaya, Return to Varanasi. Overnight in Varanasi.

Day 04:- Varanasi to Prayagraj (120kms/3hrs)
This morning post yummy breakfast at hotel, start your 4th day of Kashi Pilgrimage, by proceeding to Allahabad day tour from Varanasi, Allahabad is an ancient religious city and holds very important places in India as per Hinduism, Allahabad is now known as Prayagraj which was its ancient name, As per vedas in Hinduism, it is mentioned that Lord Brahma performed the very his first yajna in this land. Prayagraj is the place where three famous holy river of India- Ganga, Yamuna and  Saraswati meet each other and meeting point is called Triveni Sangam where you can take holy bath and later visit Bada hanuman, Patalpuri, Akshayvat, Bhardwaj Ashram, Anand bhawan old residence of famous Nehru family which is now transformed into a museum, After Completion of Places to visit in Allahabad or Itinerary for Allahabad tour. Overnight stay in Prayagraj.

Day 05:- Allahabad to Chitrakoot to Prayagraj
In morning post mouthwatering breakfast at hotel, start journey for Chitrakoot Day trip from Allahabad. Chitrakoot is a small town in the huge Bundelkhand region which falls under two states Uttar Pradesh and Madhya Pradesh - is celebrated in ballads & scriptures for its religious importance and close association with the epic Ramayan. It is believed to be the place where Lord Rama and Sita spent 12 yrs of their exile and where the Sage Atri and Sati Anasuiya meditated. Also visit Temples situated on both sides UP and MP. Post tour of places to visit in Chitrakoot and Itinerary for Chitrakoot tour. Later returned to Allahabad. Overnight in Allahabad.

Day 06:- Prayagraj to Ayodhya to Varanasi
In morning post a healthy breakfast at the hotel, Travel to Ayodhya on Varanasi Ayodhya tour package - Ayodhya also known as Saket by old name. Ayodhya is a very important religious place in India and one of the most visited places of Uttar Pradesh. Ayodhya is the birthplace of Lord Rama and the setting of the epic Ramayana. After the Supreme Court of India order in 2019, Govt of India is building a huge new Ram Janam Bhumi Temple in Ayodhya which will be a huge tourist place in Ayodhya in the coming days. On arrival in Ayodhya go for a full day sightseeing tour of Ayodhya, Visit Ram Janam bhumi, Hanuman Garhi, Kanak Bhawan, Ramkot. Post Places to visit in Ayodhya tour or best Itinerary tour for Ayodhya, drive to Kashi after Ayodhya Day tour from Varanasi. Overnight in Varanasi.

Day 07:- Varanasi Dropping
Post healthy breakfast at hotel in Varanasi, pack your bag and baggage with a promise of coming back again to Kashi for Spiritual Varanasi tour packages. Check out and Proceed to Varanasi Railway or Airport with a lot of lifelong memories of Varanasi Travel Dairies or Kashi tour packages, Reach on time to board your flight/train with a lot of sweet memories of your kashi tourist places holidays.
