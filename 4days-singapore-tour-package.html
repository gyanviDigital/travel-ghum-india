itinerary at Glance :-
Day 01:-  Singapore Arrival ​and free day​
On arrival al Changi International airport in Singapore which is one of the best airport in the world which in itself is a tourist attraction of Singapore its is a shoppers paradise, an adventurers dream, a foodies delight, Later meet and Greet our local representative at waiting area of airport and then drive to your pre-booked hotel in Singapore. After relaxing for few hours and spend the day at leisure. Overnight stay at Singapore.

Day 02:- Full Day Visit to Universal Studios in Singapore
On this morning post mouthwatering breakfast at hotel, get ready for very exciting full day visit to Universal Studios which is most popular tourist attraction of Singapore and This theme park is located inside Resort World Sentosa on Sentosa Island. It offers more than 28 rides, Several entertaining shows and many attractions across Universal seven zones. As per Universal Parks and Resorts Singapore Universal Studios is one of its kind theme Park in Asia and will be like this for next 30yrs minimum. Major things to do inside Singapore Universal Studio are Transformer ride, Shrek 4D Shows, Revenge of the Mummy x 2, Battlestar Galactica Human, Battlestar Galactica Cylon and post Lunch don't forget to go for most exciting among all is Water World. If time permits go for other shows too. Post spending memorable time at on Day trip of Singapore Universal Studios, or Post tour of top tourist attraction of Singapore, Later, return to your hotel for stay. Overnight in Singapore.

Day 03:- Singapore city tour and Singapore Sentosa Island Tour
This morning post tempting breakfast, Later Proceed for Singapore City Tour, You will be able to see most of the major Landmarks of the Singapore city on this tour perfectly suited if your time is limited. This tour include visiting vibrant Chinatown for Photo Opportunity, Since we all known Singapore was earlier Colony of British so you will see several colonial landmarks and architect in Singapore, Also pic stop incredible Little India in Singapore and the iconic idol Merlion. Your Singapore orientation tour of begins with a drive past important govt buildings such as the Civic District at the Padang, Parliament House, Supreme Court , City Hall and famous Cricket Club. Enjoy amazing view of Marina Bay when you stop for photos opportunity at Merlion Park and the Merlion, Merlion is Singapore mythological creature that is part of lion and part of fish. The sightseeing tour of Singapor continues with a visit to the Thian Hock Keng Temple as known as Tianfu Temple oldest Chinese temple dedicated Chinese sea goddess, Also visit Taoist temples one of the oldest Buddhist on Singapore island, then go Chinatown tour to a local handicraft Center. The final stop on your Singapore city half day tour is Little India visit jasmine and spices markets. This afternoon post Delicious Lunch Proceed for Half Day Sentosa tour which is one of the most visited theme park in Singapore. Its Just 15 minutes away from the Singapore city centre. Sentosa is an amazing resort island with pristine crystal clear beaches, world-class attractions, wonderful Golf course, Sea Sports and retreats. Sentosa island offers endless activities something for everyone. Enhance your Memorable Singapore experience by travelling to this island by scenic cable car, See pink dolphins play in the Dolphin Lagoon, Spend quality time at Underwater World oceanarium, see the stunning Images of Singapore story and Enjoy the sensational multi-sensory sound and light water show Songs of the Sea. if time permits visit famous S.E.A. Aquarium, Siloso Beach, Wings Of Time, Madame Tussauds Singapore. Post fun-filled Sentosa island tour or Things to do in Singapore, return back to hotel for overnight stay at Singapore.
