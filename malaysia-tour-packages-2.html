Itinerary at Glance :-
Day 01:- Arrival Kuala Lumpur and Kuala Lumpur City Tour
On arrival al KL International Airport, meet and Greet our representative at waiting area of airport and later drive to your pre-booked hotel in KL, Kuala Lumpur is beautiful capital city of Malaysia, Kl is popular for Colonial architecture and colonial buildings, Massive skyscrapers charming locals markets and a myriad of natural attractions. Kl is Divided into several districts, its main hub and main attraction centre is called the Golden Triangle which constitute Bukit Bintang, KLCC and Chinatown. After 1hr drive check-in to hotel as per checking timing. Relax for few hours and later pick up from hotel lobby and go for half day city tour of Kuala Lumpur or places to visit in Kuala Lumpur starts with visiting Petronas Twin Tower at Kuala Lumpur City Centre and Bukit Bintang area, famous Thean Hau Chinese Temple, Independence Square or Merdeka Square or Dataran Merdeka Moorish Buildings which most famous landmark of Kuala Lumpur, Explore National Monument, National Mosque, and Railway Station , don't forget to see King's Palace and Royal Selangor Pewter [largest pewter factory in the world], Post completion of places to see in Kuala Lumpur or tour of important tourist attraction in Kuala Lumpur, drive back to hotel for night stay.

Day 02:- Kuala Lumpur to Genting Highlands via Batu cave
This morning post yummy Breakfast at hotel, Pick up from hotel and go for Genting Highlands day trip from Kuala Lumpur. Genting Highland situated at 6,000 feet above sea level and only about 45mins drive from KL, It is away from the hustle and bustle of the KL city. Its is a Hill station where you will get cool fresh air, It have indoor and outdoor theme parks, 18 hole huge golf course, Genting also have biggest strawberry farm of Malaysia, All of these are available in the middle of more than 100 millions rainforest, We will stop at the Genting cable car station for your pre-booked ride and you will have breathtaking views of mountains and get down at Casino de Genting which is best in the area. On the way to visit Batu Caves which is a limestone hill with lot of caves, Batu means Rock in local Malay language. This cave is Gold plated Hindu God Lord Murgan statue at the beginning of this cave is mascot of tourism in Malaysia. This cave is popular Hindu shrine outside India. Hindu festival of Thaipusam is celebrated here in Malaysia. Post completion of Same Day Genting with Batu cave tour from Kuala Lumpur. Return back to hotel in Kl, rest of time free for exploring Kuala Lumpur markets and lively nightlife of Kuala Lumpur. Stay overnight in Kl.

Day 03​:-​ Kuala Lumpur ​to​ Sunway Lagoon ​to Kuala Lumpur​
​Post delicious ​Breakfast ​at hotel and get ready for Day trip of Sunway Lagoon from Kuala Lumpur, Pick up from hotel and proceed ​to ​Sunway Lagoon Theme Park is​ located in the township of Bandar Sunway in Subang Jaya. Spanning​ over​ ​more than ​80 acres, the lagoon draws fun seekers from all over the world with its reputation as a fascinating land of fun and excitement. The theme park is​ divided​ into 4 major themed sections​ which are ​Worlds of Adventure, Wild Wild West, Elephant Walk and Water of Africa. Experience adventure and excitement all in one place, Catch a breathtaking view as you balance yourself while walking on the World’s Longest Pedestrian Suspension Bridge measuring 428m, ​Sunway Lagoon theme park is one stop park for all type of activities ​such as water sports, fun rides, theme based rides,​ Amusements , long range of restaurants ​and recreational facilities.​ Post this day tour, Return back to hotel in Kl for night stay.​

Day 04:- Kuala Lumpur Departure
After the tasty breakfast, pack your baggage with a promise of come back again to Malaysia Truly Asia or Malaysia tourism, Proceed to different​ Kuala Lumpur​ international Airport​​ as per your flight​ timing​ on time to board your return journey flight with a lot of unforgettable memories of your Malaysia holiday Package tour or Kuala Lumpur with Genting and Sunway Lagoon​ tour Package.
