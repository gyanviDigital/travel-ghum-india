Itinerary At Glance:

This is one of the best selling tour package for Tamil Nadu, you can experience best family time on this Tamil Nadu holiday tour, It starts with arrival in Madurai which is a very famous temple town in Enchanting Tamil Nadu and Madurai temple tours covers temples such as Meenakshi Temple, Thirupparamkunram Murugan Temple, etc, Next stop is Kodaikanal which is 2nd most famous hill station of Tamil Nadu after Ooty, Kodaikanal offer you tour of many beautiful lakes, waterfalls and many amazing parks. Third stop on this tour is ​Ooty ​which is the most visited hill station in TamilNadu and the most famous hill station of South India. This Tamil Nadu hill station tour package includes a tour of all major hill stations in the state, Pick up is from Madurai airport and Departure is from Coimbatore which is 2nd largest city of Tamil Nadu.

Itinerary at Glance :-
Day 01:- Madurai Arrival and Local
On arrival at Ancient city Madurai airport or railway station, our friendly representative will meet and greet our representative outside exit gate, Then drive to Madurai city and on arrival check-in to your pre-booked hotel, later go for top places to visit in Madurai -Start by visiting 6th century Sri Meenakshi Sundareswarar Temple situated in heart of Madurai city and this temple was rebuilt in 14th century, this famous Hindu temple is situated on bank of the River Vaigai and temple is dedicated to goddess Menakshi a form of goddess Parvati, Later explore 17th Century Thirumalai Nayak palace built by King Tirumala Nayaka and it is best example of Italian and Rajput styles of architecture in India, 6th century Subramanya Swamy Temple or Thiruparankundram temple in Thiruparankundram which is 8kms from Madurai was built by Pandyas temple is dedicated to Lord Murugan, also visit Alagar kovil situated on sacred Alagar Hills- this temple is dedicated to lord Vishnu and At last visit Gandhi museum in Madurai. Post completion best things to do in Madurai. Stay overnight in Madurai.

Day 02:- Madurai to Kodaikanal (120kms/3-4hrs)
After tempting breakfast, check out from the Hotel in Madurai and drive to Kodaikanal from Madurai. Kodaikanal is the 2nd most popular hill station of Tamil Nadu after Ooty. Kodaikanal is a Tamil name which means "The Gift of the Forest". On arrival, check-in to the hotel. Later visit the Bryant Park which is famous botanical garden of Kodaikanal spread in 20.5 acres and Build in 1908, it homes almost 325 varieties of trees and 740 varieties of Roses and Kodaikanal Lake is manmade lake build 1863, it is star shape and spread in 60 acres, Kodaikanal is main tourist attraction of Kodaikanal here you can hire Rowboats, Pedalos, you can spend your evening walking around Kodaikanal lake which is blessed with mother nature in and around. Overnight stay at Hotel in Kodaikanal.  

Day 03:- Kodaikanal sightseeing tour
This morning post delicious breakfast, Get ready for the most exciting day of the tour, go for best Sightseeing and Tourist Attractions in Kodaikanal. The Coaker's walk is named after Lt Coaker who constructed it in 1872. It is a 1km Pedestrian paved path it goes around steep slopes of the southern part of Kodaikanal, Green Valley view is view point of Kodaikanal from here you will get breathtaking panoramic view of Kodaikanal city, Pillar rocks in Kodaikanal is view point from where you can see mountains and three giant shape rock pillar which are 400fts high, If timer permits You may also visit Chettiar park, Shenbaganur museum and Silver Cascade waterfall. Post places to visit in Kodaikanal tour return back to the hotel for overnight stay at Kodaikanal.

Day 04:- Kodaikanal to Ooty  (260kms/7-8 hrs)
This Morning after a tasty breakfast, check out the hotel and drive to Ooty which is the most visited hill station of South India and is situated in the Nilgiri hills, also called The Blue Mountains. Ooty greets its visitors by Beautiful lakes with fresh water, Mighty mountains, thick dense forests, sprawling grasslands, eucalyptus trees and Never ending miles of tea gardens. On the way, you may visit Bandipur National Park or Mudumalai National Park and Pykara Waterfall is a famous picnic spot. On arrival at Ooty, check in to the hotel, after having lunch at local restaurants and rest of time free for leisure and shopping. Overnight stay in Ooty.

Day 05:- Ooty to Coonoor to Ooty
Post yummy buffet breakfast at hotel, Get ready for fun-filled sightseeing tour of Ooty and Coonoor start by visiting 20kms from Ooty, go for famous Coonoor Tea Gardens, Lambs rock view point, Dolphin Nose is hidden gem it provide breathtaking view of beautiful Catherine Falls, Post Important places to visit in Coonoor. After having lunch at local restaurants, Proceed to Ooty (by toy train - optional) or Car. On arrival go for Ooty Sightseeing tour star with Iconic Doddabetta Peak is highest point in Ooty and 2637 meters above sea level, Then visit Botanical Gardens, a famous tourist spot of Ooty, also visit Ooty lake and Rose Garden. Post Important tourist places in Ooty which is also known as Queen of hill stations, return to the hotel, Overnight in Ooty.

Day 06:- Kodaikanal to Coimbatore drop (100kms/3hrs)
This morning post tempting breakfast, pack your bag and baggage with a promise of coming back again, Check-out on your for Ooty Kodaikanal tour package. Proceed towards Coimbatore Airport/Railway station on time to board your flight for onward journey to home with a lot of sweet memories of your Madurai-Ooty-Kodaikanal tour package.
