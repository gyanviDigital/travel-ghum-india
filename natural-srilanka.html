Itinerary At Glance:

Sri Lanka is taken as one of the finest island country of World, Sri Lanka is truly blessed by Mother Nature, You will find a big rock in the middle of Jungle at Sigiriya, several herds of Wild Asian elephants, Sri Lanka is more like paradise to all nature lovers. Here you will have a huge range of ecosystems, Sri Lanka holiday tour is all together a complete package for all age groups of travellers. Sri Lanka offers beautiful coastal beaches, huge dry forests and several rainforests. This 4N/5Days Sri Lanka tour package offers tour of four important tourist destination of Sri Lanka which include Kandy Sightseeing tour with Ramboda waterfall , Nuwara Eliya sightseeing tour with Sigiriya, top beaches of Sri Lanka with Bentota beach tour and at end Colombo city tour which is capital of Sri Lanka. Itinerary at Glance :-
Day 01:- Colombo Arrival and Colombo to Kandy via Pinnawala (140kms/4hrs)
This day on arrival at Bandaranaike International Airport in Colombo on your 4N/5D Sri Lanka tour package or Kany-Nuwara Eliya- Bentota and Colombo tour package, Post completion of immigration formalities, Meet and greet our representative at airport waiting area, Later drive to Kand, Kandy is the 2nd largest city of Sri Lanka and its situated on a Plateau and it is Surrounded by never ending tea plantation or Tea Garden in Kandy and also surrounded by Mountains. On the way visit Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage which is an orphanage for Elephants and its largest captive breeding place for Wild Asian elephants in the World. It is Located in Pinnawala Village and it has become super famous among tourists ever since beautiful photos were getting viral on social media. Later visit Spice and Herb Gardens of Sri Lanka in Mawanella. Post spice garden tour of Sri Lanka continue your journey to Kandy, On arrival check-in to the hotel and after relaxing for a few hours go for things to do in Kandy start with visiting Famous Gem Museum of Kandy, Then in evening witness beautifully choreographed traditional Sri Lankan Dance show. Laterâ€‹, return back to the hotel, Overnight in Kandy.

Day 02:- Kandy Sightseeing tour and Kandy to Nuwara Eliya via Ramboda waterfall  (80kms/2.5hrs)
Morning post delicious breakfast at hotel, check out and go for tour of Places to visit in Kandy or Places to see in Kandy , Kandy is famous among traveller for its religious sites and Colonial buildings. Starts by visiting Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic or Sri Dalada Maligawa is a buddhist temple which houses the relic of the tooth of Buddha and located inside the former royal palace complex in Kandy, Visit famous the Botanical Garden in Kandy or Peradeniya Gardens - it homes to more than 4000 species of trees and plants, Explore The Streets of Kandy you will lot of  with old colonial buildings and street full of vendors, Visit The Bahiravokanda Vihara Buddha Statue in Kandy- it is 88 foot high and biggest Buddha statue in Sri Lanka, situated on hill top and Panoramic view Kandy city from here is simply amazing. Last but not least, Kandy Lake spends some quality time here. It is considered as the heart and soul of the city. Visit Batiks factory famous for local Sri Lankan Handicrafts, Post completion of tour of top places to visit in Kandy, drive to next exciting destination Nuwara Eliya, On the way visit Ramboda waterfall which is 11th highest fall of Sri Lanka at 109 meters high, it is in Pussellawa area on A5 highway of Sri Lanka. On arrival at Nuwara Eliya, Check-in to your pre-booked hotel. Overnight stay in Nuwara Eliya.

Optional tours from Kandy:- Kandy to Ella Train- Train takes you through never ending stunning tea plantation hills, it is regarded as one of the world's most beautiful train journey & Day trip to Sigiriya from Kandy - Lion Rock situated here and it is UNESCO world heritage sites in Sri Lanka and a must-do in Sri Lanka.

Day 03:- Nuwara Eliya sightseeing tour and Nuwara Eliya to Bentota (230kms/6hrs)
This morning post yummy breakfast at Hotel, Check out and go for places to visit in Nuwara Eliya or things to do in Nuwara Eliya . Nuwara Eliya is famous as Little England of Sri Lanka, It is cooler in climate than other parts of Sri Lanka and more suitable for tea production in Sri Lanka. Start you places to see in Nuwara Eliya by visiting Beautiful 18 holes Golf Course of Nuwara Eliya which was founded in 1889 and it is one of the oldest golf course of Asia, also visit Race course and Strawberry farm in Nuwara Eliya, don't forget to Explore New Zealand Farm and Seeta Amman Temple dedicated to goddess Sita wife of Lord Ram and  situated on the hills of Nuwara Eliya. Spend quality time at Gregory Lake and you can opt for many sports activities at lake. You may go for a walk around World’s End which is situated in the Horton Plains National Park which is a UNESCO world heritage site. Post tour top place to see in Nuwara Eliya, drive to Bentota, On arrival check-in to hotel and evening free to spend time at beach. Overnight at Bentota in Sri Lanka.

Day 04:- Bentota sightseeing tour and Bentota to Colombo (50 Kms/ 3 Hr)
This morning post tempting breakfast at hotel , Check out and later visit Bentota beach is one the finest and beautiful beach of Sri Lanka, This beach have something to offer for everyone, Bentota beach is safest beach for swimmers all around the year, Bentota is famous for water sports activities for tourist here you can go for water surfing, Banana boat ride, snorkelling. water skiing, etc. Go for Turtle hatchery at Bentota, Go cruising at Madu river boat ride safari to visit Cinnamon Island where you can see Buddhist temple, thick mangrove forest, See unique style of Fishing as well as prawn trapping being practised by local inhabitants of this island. Post completion of Places to visit in Bentota or things to do in Bentota, drive to Colombo capital city of Sri Lanka, It is the largest city of Sri Lanka and it is a Cosmopolitan city. On arrival check-in to hotel and later go for things to do in Colombo or places to see in Colombo or Top tourist attraction of Colombo by visiting Gangaramaya Buddhist Temple, Independence Memorial Hall and Galle Face beach, Later you may go for Colombo shopping tour Visiting Odel Cotton collection, Beverly Street, Majestic city, Noritake Store in Colombo. Post tour of places to visit in Colombo, Return back to hotel for Overnight stay at your hotel in Colombo.

Day 05:- Colombo Airport drop
After the tasty breakfast, pack your baggage with a promise of coming back again to So Sri Lanka or Wonder of Asia Sri Lanka, Proceed to Colombo Airport on time to board your return journey flight with a lot of unforgettable memories of your Sri Lanka Holiday Package tour.
