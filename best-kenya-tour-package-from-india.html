Itinerary at Glance:-
Day 01:- Nairobi Arrival
On arrival at Jomo Kenyatta International Airport in Nairobi, Kenya. Meet and greet our friendly representative, later drive to your pre-booked hotel in comfortable cab, on the way witness beauty of Nairobi. Check-in to hotel and overnight stay in Nairobi.

Day 02:- Nairobi to Masai Mara
After having yummy breakfast at hotel, Check out and go for tour of Masai Mara Game Reserve or Masai Mara National Reserve which is one of the most famous tourist destinations in Kenya. This reserve is situated in the Great Rift Valley in mostly open grassland. This journey is simply beautiful. This National park is named after Maasai People to show respect to them they are the real local ancestral inhabitants of this place and mara means spotted in Maasai Language. On arrival at Masai Mara National Reserve check-in to your pre-booked Lodge and in afternoon post lunch go for game viewing at park, you may spot many spices of flora and fauna. As per experts park have some 95 species of mammals, reptiles and amphibians and over 400 species of birds in the reserve. You may spot several wildlife species such as African elephants, leopard, cheetah, Roan antelope, wildebeests, African buffalo, jackals and many more. Overnight stay at Masai Mara National Reserve.

Day 03:- Masai Mara game viewing (two safaris morning and afternoon)
After delicious breakfast at lodge, full day is reserve for game-viewing in the reserve. Wildlife in the reserve tends to be mostly concentrated on the western escarpment of the reserve. The Masai Mara is taken as the jewel of Kenya’s for wildlife viewing tour. Masai Mara reserve have two wildlife tours one is in morning and one in evening. This reserve is famous because all Big Five Lion, Leopard, Elephant, Cape Buffalo and Rhinoceros are found here throughout the year. Apart from this Hippopotami and crocodiles are available in large no. in the Mara and Talek rivers. Other important animals present here are Hyenas, cheetahs, jackals, and bat-eared foxes. Best place to spot animal in the Masai Mara reserve is plains between the Mara River and the Esoit Siria Escarpment mainly Lions and Cheetah spotted here in good numbers. Post wildlife safari tour of Masai Mara national reserve. Overnight stay in Lodge.

Day 04:- Masai Mara to Lake Naivasha and Lake Naivasha to Lake Nakuru national park
Post finishing your tasty breakfast, Check out and  drive towards Lake Naivasha which is a freshwater lake situated in Nakuru County and it is part of the great rift valley. On arrival enjoy your boat riding in this beautiful lake where you may spot Hippos. Post boat ride in Naivasha Lake, drive toward other beautiful Lake Nakuru which is one of the Rift Valley soda lakes at an elevation of 1,754 m. On arrival here go for bird watching at Lake Nakuru National Park, a place blessed with natural beauty. This lake has a large amount of algae, which attracts flamingos in large numbers. On your bird watching tour at Lake Nakuru in Kenya try to spot two beautiful creature Pink flamingos and White Rhinos here. Post appetising lunch, go for the Baboon cliff to engage yourself in a breathtaking game-drive. This is hugely popular view point in the Lake Nakuru national park. As it is located at a higher altitude, one can have spectular view of the surrounding from here. Apart from spotting pink flamingo you can also witness other birds because around 450 avifauna species lives here, overnight in Lake Naivasha/ Lake Nakuru.

Day 05:- Lake Nakuru to Sweetwater
Once you have your tempting breakfast at hotel, Check out and continue your tour towards Sweetwaters. On the way stop at beautiful Nyahururu Falls, also commonly known as Thompson’s Falls. Later on don't forget to stop at the Equator you can click as much pictures you want here. On arrival at Sweetwaters, the check-in at the Camp. Post delectable lunch, visit Famous and one of its kind The Sweetwaters Chimpanzee Sanctuary which was established in 1993, Keeping mind to not only provide home to endanger Chimpanzee species but also provide stress free environment to rescued, orphaned and abused chimps. Overnight at Sweetwater camps.

Day 06:- Nairobi Airport Drop
This morning post mouthwatering at camp, pack your bag and baggages with lot unforgettable memories of Kenya Holiday trip from India as well as Kenya wildlife safari tour from India, Check out and drop to airport as per flight time, Return home with sweet memories of your kenya tour packages from hyderabad, kenya tour packages from Delhi, kenya tour packages from bangalore, kenya tour packages from mumbai, kenya tour packages from Chennai.
