Overview

Embark on a splendid 14-night/15-day Rajasthan tour with Varanasi, meticulously crafted through Ghum India Ghum. This immersive adventure commences in the vibrant heart of Delhi, leading you through an exciting exploration of Rajasthan, Orchha, Khajuraho, and Varanasi. Experience the rich tapestry of northern India,  for its vibrant culture and majestic landmarks.

 

Delve into the grandeur of Rajasthan, where ancient marvels like Samode Palace, Amber Fort, Mehrangarh Fort, and Town Palace await your discovery. Traverse the desert of Ranthambore National Park, the dwelling house of the Royal Bengal Tiger.

 

The adventure continues onwards to Orchha and Khajuraho, wherein the breathtaking temples, which include Kandariya Mahadeo and Chaunsat Yogini, will captivate your senses. Sooner or later, immerse yourself in the non-secular air of mystery in Varanasi, the oldest-residing city in the world. Witness the enchanting Ganga Aarti, cruise alongside the Ganges River, and discover the revered Vishwanath Temple and Manikarnika Ghat.

 

Go get your bags pack, to experience the essence of Rajasthan Tour With Varanasi, designed for every enthusiast of India's wealthy heritage and natural splendor. Don't pass over this brilliant opportunity to create enduring memories with us! Book your adventure with Ghum India Ghum these days.

Itinerary:

01
Day 01: Arrival in Delhi

As you step off the plane at the Delhi airport, our representative will warmly welcome you at exit gate #5 for the Rajasthan Tour with Varanasi. They'll guide you through the airport and outside, where the sun will cast a golden hue over the metropolis and modern city of Delhi. Check in at your pre-booked hotel, amidst the city buzz, awaits your arrival, and promises you a healthy comfort  and relaxation after your journey.

02
Day 02: Full-day Tour in Delhi

After a filling breakfast, you will embark on the 2nd day Rajasthan Tour with Varanasi, which will offer a full day of learning about Delhi's vibrant traditions and extensive history. Explore the historic treasures of Old Delhi, such as the majestic Jama Masjid, the historic Red Fort, Chandni chowk, Raj Ghat and the Birla Mandir . After that, travel to New Delhi to explore the architectural wonders of the Qutub Minar, Rashtrapati Bhavan, India Gate, Lotus Temple and Gurudwara Bangla Sahib (if time permits).

03
Day 03: Drive to Jaipur

On the 3rd day of the Rajasthan Tour with Varanasi, you’ll head for a drive to the beautiful scenic adventure of Jaipur, where the sandstone hues of the pink town will await. The streets of Jaipur unfold like a ribbon via the nation-state, echoing glimpses of rural existence against a backdrop of rolling hills. As you’ll arrive in Jaipur, the aroma of spices and the vibrant colors of the bustling bazaars will welcome you.

04
Day 4: Full Day Tour to Jaipur

On the 4th day of the Rajasthan Tour with Varanasi, you will explore Jaipur's charming records as you walk through its historical streets. Have a visit to The City Palace, Albert HAll, Jantar Mantar and Amber Fort, which locations were full of tales of the past. Then we will move to Jal Mahal, which will sit on the water like a magical fortress. On the other hand, you will visit the Hawa Mahal which is one of the famous glimpses of Jaipur and will have lots of home windows that will gush the secrets and techniques. Jaipur will be a metropolis palace where kings used to stay, and you will see their grand halls and gardens there. It will be like walking through a treasure trove of Jaipur's past.

05
Day 05: Drive to Ranthambore

Leaving the town's colorful colorations, you’ll head towards Ranthambore to get the thrilling experience. Enjoy the adventure in Ranthambore, wherein nature will reign supreme. The panorama will give way to rugged terrain and dense foliage in the flora and fauna sanctuary in Ranthambore. Anticipation will build, as now it will be time to prepare for encounters with the majestic tigers and myriad natural worlds that call home for Royal Bengal Tigers.

06
Day 06: Full-Day Visit in Ranthambore

On the 6th day of the Rajasthan Tour with Varanasi, enjoy the early sunrise scene over the Ranthambore sky, casting a golden glow over the clouds. In the crisp morning air, prepare yourself to set out on a 3 hour jungle safari in an open jeep, where your senses will be alive with the opportunity of spotting cagey creatures in the jungle environment. As the day unfolds, you will witness nature's drama spread before your eyes; each moment here will be a testament to the gorgeous splendor of the wild.

07
Day 07: Drive Agra via Fatehpur Sikri

On the 7th day of the Rajasthan Tour with Varanasi, your adventure will continue in the direction of Agra, punctuated by a planned visit to Fatehpur Sikri, a ghost metropolis frozen in time. The historical sandstone structures whisper tales of Mughal beauty, ephemeral nature, and energy. Around evening, you will arrive in Agra, followed by a pleasing visit to the Taj Mahal, a symbol of everlasting love whose ethereal beauty will be heightened by the gentle glow of the setting sun.

08
Day 08: Full-Day Visit of Agra

On the 8th day of your Rajasthan tour with Varanasi, wake up at Agra, a city steeped in records that invites us to resolve its secrets. In the red walls of Agra Fort, the legacy of the Mughal emperors will unfold Diwan-I-Am (hall for public audience), and  Diwan-i-Khas (hall for private audience); and the tricky architecture will be a testament to their grandeur. In your well-planned timeline if time permits close by, the infant Taj Mahal will stand as a precursor to its grander sibling, providing a glimpse into the architectural marvels that will decorate the metropolis's landscape. As the day draws to a close, visit the Mehtab Bagh, which will unveil its beauty too.

09
Day 09: Travel to Jhansi by Train

On the 9th day of your Rajasthan tour with Varanasi, you will board a fast air-conditioned train journey from Agra to Jhansi, then a drive to Orchha, where time will appear to be still amidst the historical ruins. As you reach the cobblestone streets, the echoes of centuries past will stir the air, each step a testimony to the enduring legacy of a bygone era. Amidst the palatial splendor and ancient temples visit the laxmi narayan temple, jehangir mahal and rajmahal. The city Orchha will invite us to embark on a journey through time in which history and background will converge in an enchanting tapestry of lifestyle and subculture.

10
Day 10: Drive to Khajuraho

In the beginning of the 10th day of the Rajasthan Tour with Varanasi, your trip adventure will lead you towards Khajuraho, where ancient temples stand as silent sentinels to a bygone generation, which are best world artistic wonders, as these temples are devoted to the several hindu pantheon of gods are covered in cravings that are a paean to life and love. The road will wind through verdant landscapes, each flip revealing glimpses of rural life against the backdrop of lush greenery. As we arrive in Khajuraho, the complex carvings and towering spires of the temples will beckon, a testament to the creative prowess of historic civilizations.

11
Day 11: Full-Day Visit to Khajuraho

On the 11th day of the tour, Khajuraho will help unveil its architectural wonders as we wander through its historic places and surrounding area. The intricately carved sculptures will come alive, each telling a tale of affection, devotion, and divine grace. Amidst the tranquil atmosphere of the temples, also explore Khajuraho Eastern and Western Group of Temple with main attractions - Parsvanath Temple, and the Adinath Temple. you will be transported to a realm in which the mundane will fade away, leaving behind a feel of Marvel City.

Another exciting additional tour for this day on direct payment post tourist place to visit in Khajuraho you may opt for the Evening Panna Jungle safari tour from Khajuraho, Panna is only 1hr drive from Khajuraho. Panna National Park is the 22nd National Park of India and it was declared in 1992. Evening Jungle Safari in Panna will let you experience the dense ambiance of the forest and give you a chance to encounter the wildest ranges of mammals, reptiles, and several species of migratory birds, flora, and fauna. Other than this, you will even be able to get the chance to spot the tiger species at very close quarters, Later you will return back to the hotel for a Night stay in Khajuraho.

 

12
Day 12: Drive to Varanasi

Now by dropping you at Khajuraho airport to board a flight to Varanasi adventure, the religious coronary heart of India, where historical rituals and timeless traditions will converge along the banks of the Ganges. As you explore the city, you will get to know that the town will be famous for its secrets of tradition and devotion. Also visit the main glimpse of Varanasi that includes Harishchandra Ghat and Manikarnaka Ghat, which is also known as Burning Ghat. As twilight descends, the ghats will come alive with the flickering flames of aarti, a symphony of devotion that will resonate for a long time.

13
Day 13: Full-day Visit in Varanasi

As soon as you wake up, you will see that Varanasi will awaken with the primary light of dawn, casting a golden glow over the ancient city. You will also book a boat ride to enjoy the beautiful sunrise of the ancient city of world Kashi. The ganga ghats will offer a glimpse into the undying rituals that have defined that area for Aarti. Later in the afternoon, visit the Dhamek Stupa and Thai temples. Also if you wander through the narrow alleys, Sarnath will welcome us with its tranquil atmosphere, a testament to the teachings of the Buddha that will continue to inspire seekers of fact and enlightenment.

14
Day 14: Varanasi to Delhi

On the 14th day of the Rajasthan Tour With Varanasi, your adventure will come to a complete circle as we bid farewell to Varanasi and make the way back to Delhi, where the vibrant tapestry of India's capital will await. As we traverse the bustling streets with the recollections of your journey, every second will be a testament to the richness and variety of India's cultural heritage.

15
Day 15: Departure from Delhi

On the last day of your Rajasthan tour with Varanasi, you will make your departure. With a coronary heart full of recollections and a soul enriched by the experiences of this awesome journey. As you board a flight, you will hear the echoes of laughter, the warmth of friendship, and the promise of future adventures yet to come. Till we meet once more. Revise your spending moments during these 15 days and reach out to us with your valuable thoughts and reviews regarding Ghum India Ghum.
