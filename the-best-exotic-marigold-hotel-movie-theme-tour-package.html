Itinerary At Glance:

Directed by John Madden featuring legendary Judi Dench, Maggie Smith, Bill Nighy, Tom Wilkinson. British retirees citizens travel to India to take up residence in what they believe is a newly restored hotel. They found it less Luxurious than advertised slowly over the time Marigold hotel started charming them in an unexpected way. Our Best Exotic Marigold Hotel Tour of India is blessed on the footsteps of the movie in India and includes a stay in the same Marigold hotel whose real name is Ravla Khempur hotel in Udaipur. Our Best Exotic Marigold Hotel Tour Itinerary is Inspired by best of the Best Exotic Marigold Hotel movies in India and informative BBC documentaries that followed after that. On this The Best Exotic Marigold Hotel Film Location you can witness rich cultural of India on your Delhi Agra Jaipur Udaipur tour with Marigold hotel stay tour or marigold hotel tours. You will kick off with Sightseeing of both New and Old Delhi, See iconic Taj Mahal and Magnificent Agra fort in Agra, Stunning tour of Pink city of India Jaipur and Exploring unexplored parts of Udaipur along with Khempur village tour which is real selling point of this movie, Post completion of this tour you will also see several changes in yourself too, what shown in movie you can experience in real on this tour. Khempur is a small village situated 50kms from Udaipur, Hotel Ravla Khempur is in this village only whose name changed to Hotel Marigold in the movie. Kempur tourism is real example of rural tourism in India, here you can see rural life of India from nearby and rural India is real India.

Itinerary at Glance:-
Day 01:- ​Delhi arrival​​ and ​Pick up 
On arrival in Delhi at Indira Gandhi International Airport, Post immigration formalities meet and greet our representative outside Exit gate#5 on your marigold hotel tours, later transfer to pre-booked hotel in Delhi. Stay overnight at a hotel in Delhi.

Day 02:- Delhi Sightseeing tour
Post Delicious breakfast, we explore both Old and New Delhi on marigold tours india. First go for an old Delhi tour and Take a rickshaw ride through Chandni Chowk bazaar and discover highlights of the old city by visiting largest and oldest mosque in India - Jama Masjid, Raj Ghat-the site of Gandhi’s cremation and where an eternal flame now burns 24*7 in his honour, Take outside tour of Red Fort a Unesco site in Delhi. Post Lunch, go for sightseeing of New Delhi starts with Raj Path tour, Visit India Gate monument, Rashtrapati Bhavan, Parliament house (from Outside) and later visit Another two UNESCO world heritage sites Humayun’s Tomb and Qutub Minar in Delhi. Post tour of Place to visit in Delhi, return back to your hotel for a relaxing stay in Delhi.

Day 03:- Delhi to Agra​ (230kms/4hrs)​
Post yummy breakfast at hotel, proceed to Agra on your india marigold hotel tour. On arrival check-in to your pre-booked hotel in Agra, First go for Afternoon tour of Massive Agra fort built by Red Stone and is Unesco site in Agra. Later post lunch enjoy an evening tour of the Magnificent Taj Mahal which is India’s most visited and iconic attraction. The monument is built of white marble from Makrana in Rajasthan and was constructed by Mughal ruler Shah Jahan in memory of his beloved wife and is the most fantastic monument dedicated to love that the world has ever seen, Taj Mahal is another and most beautiful UNESCO world heritage site in Agra. It is a one of the out of the world experience to watch the mesmerising sunset over this romantic site​-​we get to witness this before we return to our hotel. If time permits visit Mehtab Bagh in Agra. Stay overnight in Agra.
Note :- Taj mahal closed on friday

Day 04:- Agra to Jaipur via Abhaneri and evening tea at CASTLE KANOTA
In this morning after a healthy breakfast in Agra, checkout from hotel and drive to Jaipur en-route visit Abhaneri which is 156kms from Agra, On your Delhi-agra-jaipur with Abhaneri or Golden Triangle tour with Abhaneri. Abhaneri which is famous for ancient Chand Baori stepwell. On the way visit the village of Abhaneri and admire the priceless beauty of Chand Baori step well, which famously featured in the blockbuster film ‘Batman: The Dark Knight Rises’. It is the most visited stepwell in India and Is Most famous for its unique architectural style which includes a deep four-sided well with a large temple on the backside of the building, It is made of more than 3500 narrow steps and startling 13 storeys. Chand Baori is an ancient stepwell of Rajasthan constructed just opposite to sacred Harshat Mata Temple in the 800 AD, the stepwell was built by Chanda of the famous Nikumbh Empire with keeping mind of solving the water crisis at that time. Post Abhaneri tour or Chand Baori proceed to next destination Jaipur. The city earned its nickname when the Old City was painted pink by then​ ​Maharaja to welcome the Prince of Wales (soon to be King Edward VII) to Jaipur in 1876. Relax and enjoy the sights as we travel, then enjoy a ​authentic ​luscious dinner of Rajasthani cuisine on arrival. Stay overnight at a hotel in Jaipur. Hotel Narayan Niwas

Day 05:- Jaipur city tour
Post hygienic breakfast at hotel in Jaipur, Enjoy a full day of sightseeing in Jaipur on your marigold hotel holiday tours, beginning with a visit to the impressive Amber Fort. This palace isBuilt over four tough layers and sits beside the shining Maota Lake. We arrive at the site by jeep and explore this huge Palace UNESCO site in Jaipur which is situated on the outskirts of Jaipur city, before continuing to a local flower market. Then, we explore Jal Mahal situated in the middle of Man Sagar lake, take a selfie outside of Hawa Mahal, Later visit the City Palace  and visit its well preserved museum of regal clothing and textiles. Then we continue to the another UNESCO site in Jaipur is Jantar Mantar which is a space observatory. This evening, explore the hustling and bustling local bazaar and take part in a traditional cooking demonstration class at your hotel. After​ you have​ finished ​cooking ​dinner, enjoy feasting on our delicious creations. Stay overnight at a hotel in Jaipur. Hotel Pearl  Heritage

Day 06:- Jaipur to Udaipur
Early morning after breakfast start 6th day of your Real Exotic Marigold Hotel Tour by proceeding to Udaipur. On arrival in the Udaipur city by evening, if possible try some Indian chai hot tea from a local street vendor, before we go to enjoy a thrilling tuk-tuk ride through the city’s congested lanes of Udaipur, stopping by to the most important sites. You can relive the scene from the Popular ‘Best Exotic Marigold Hotel’ as we stop at a lakeside restaurant for a drink - the same way as Evelyn and Madge do in the film. Later in the evening we drove out of the city to enjoy a stunning sunset view and panoramic view of Udaipur city from the hill-top iconic Monsoon Palace. This remarkable site was featured in 1983 in hit James Bond film ‘Octopuses’, starring legendary Roger Moore. Stay overnight at a hotel in Udaipur. Hotel Jagat Niwas Palace

Day 07:- Udaipur city tour & Udaipur to Khempur village (43kms/1hr)
This morning post mouthwatering breakfast at hotel in Udaipur, Start 7th day on your The Best Exotic Marigold Hotel Film Tour package. First start your tour with enjoying a boat ride on Lake Pichola this morning for witnessing breathtaking views of the forested Aravalli Mountains range. You will have free time to roam around the four islands on this lake, Later we proceed to the famous Khempur village tour which is 42kms away from Udaipur. This village is real home to the original ‘Best Exotic Marigold Hotel’, where most of the iconic movie was filmed. On arrival we check-in to this iconic hotel whose real name is the Ravla Khempur hotel and enjoy an evening stroll with our professional guide through the village. Stay overnight at The best exotic Marigold Hotel. This Night stay going to be with you as a lifelong memory. Ravla Khempur hotel

Day 08:- Full stay free for Ravla Khempur hotel stay, Khempur village (The Best Exotic Marigold Hotel)
Early in the morning take a traditional and iconic oxen cart ride to see Khempur village and its surrounding area to experience the relaxed pace of village life. Khempur village is the brand ambassador of rural tourism in India and ecotourism in India, situated approx 50kms from Udaipur city which is district headquarter, spread over an area of 1048 hectares of land and has a population of approx 2038 peoples, this village has around 485 houses. On your Kempur Village tour you will see real rural India life from nearby and you can understand local tradition and culture better. Post tour return to hotel have you tempting breakfast at Ravla Khempur hotel. Rest of the day free for Relaxing. In Evening watch your favourite Movie " The Best Exotic Marigold Hotel" at the real location of the film. Evening enjoy Dinner. Stay overnight at a Ravla Khempur hotel in Udaipur. Ravla Khempur hotel

Day 09:- Udaipur to Delhi  (By Flight)
This morning post tasty breakfast at the hotel, it's time to say goodbye to this iconic The Best Exotic Marigold hotel  and check out from the hotel and drive to Udaipur international airport to board the flight to Delhi. On arrival in Delhi pick up and drop to Delhi hotel, rest of the day free for leisure. Overnight in Delhi

Day 10:- Delhi Departure
Today after appetizing breakfast at hotel in Delhi, your Indian adventure with Hotel Exotic Marigold tour package comes to an end - unlike the characters shown in the film, you have a plane to catch! your tour ends with an included drop to the Delhi international airport as per your flight timing. Return home with lifelong memories of India and Exotic Marigold hotel tour in India.

THE BEST EXOTIC MARIGOLD HOTEL (FOR THE ELDERLY AND BEAUTIFUL)

The Best Exotic Marigold Hotel in real life , the Ravla Khempur, is not actually located in Jaipur. It’s over 200 miles away on the outskirts of Udaipur, nestled in the countryside. It is 17th century property which offers 12 en-suite rooms, all of which have an equestrian touch (the owner, Hemant Singh, is a horse enthusiast).

THE VICEROY CLUB – CASTLE KANOTA

Castle Kanota, built in 1872 and it is spread over 8 acres of land, was the venue for the Viceroy Club and several of Celia Imrie and Ronald Pickup’s scenes in the film. It’s located outside Jaipur capital city of Rajasthan, about half an hour’s drive away. This hotel offers 18 spacious rooms and a built-in museum, all decorated in a traditional heritage style and evoking the grandeur of princely Rajasthan. Castle Kanota is locate at peaceful neighbourhood you can use it as base to explore the Pink City, or those who like to stay more centrally can visit this place for high tea, sundowners or a special dinner.  Viceroy Club Best Exotic Marigold Hotel.

THE SECOND BEST MARIGOLD HOTEL

Hotel Pearl Palace in Jaipur, was the venue for the Second Best Marigold Hotel, and it was voted as India’s ‘Most Romantic Hotel’ by Trip Advisor users in 2015. Colourful rooms, and raise the bar for budget digs in India. The properties rooftop restaurant, Peacock, is one of the most popular in Jaipur.

UDAIPUR (Jagat Niwas Palace)

In the film at one point all the characters travel to Udaipur. This fairy-tale like city, dotted with marble palaces and lakes surrounded by a ring of hills, was founded by Maharana Udai Singh in 1559. Udaipur is a romantic city is dominated by the massive City Palace, which overlooks Lake Pichola and its dreamy island palaces, including the Lake Palace seen in the film.  The Lake Palace is more than 250 years old, and now it is one of the luxury hotel run by the Taj group. Some scenes were also shot at the Jagat Niwas Palace, where the characters had rooftop drinks.
