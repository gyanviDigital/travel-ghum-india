Explore Best Same Day Trips From Delhi

Sarika ChauhanMay 30, 2024May 30, 2024

Exploring a same day trip from Delhi is not just a journey via roads but also an unforgettable memory lifetime. When the topic is about best same day trips from Delhi, Ghum India Ghum is the leading travel agency. One-day trips are a boon for many who want a refreshing start at the beginning of the week.

This extensive guide will assist you through every step of your day trips from Delhi. You can easily explore fun one-day destinations with friends and family near Delhi, rather than high budgets, disrupting your daily routines.

Let’s check out the most popular one-day trips from Delhi:

1. Jaipur

In just a 5-hour ride you can visit the marvellous ‘Pink City’ if you can plan your day to see the most popular ones. Starting from the magnificent Amber Fort, followed by the majestic Hawa Mahal and the unique amalgamation of ancient architecture of temples will make your journey unforgettable. The elegant sight of Lake Palace with coloured stones and deep blue water will undoubtedly make you another admirer.

This Jaipur day trip from Delhi will mesmerise your inner soul and certainly lighten up your boring weekend.

How to reach

Delhi has three different routes to Jaipur. These routes are via NE4, NH21 and NH48. You can get it via NE4 and NH21 in just 5 hours and 10 mins, but NH48 would be the fastest way, as it is only 276 km away.

2. Bharatpur

The second one on the list is an off-beat destination, heaven for bird lovers. While planning for Bharatpur day trips from Delhi, you must add Lohagarh Fort, Laxman Mandir, Chawad Devi temple, and Ganga Mandir. The Keoladeo National Park, a UNESCO World Heritage site, is home to hundreds of bird species. Your trip to Bharatpur will be incomplete if you don’t visit this bird sanctuary.

How to reach:

You can take the Yamuna Expressway or NH44 to travel from Delhi to Bharatpur. It will take approximately 4 hours to reach Bharatpur via Yamuna Expressway. The 215 km route of NH44 will take you to your destination in just 4 hours and 17 mins.

3. Agra

Agra, known for ‘The Taj Mahal,’ symbolises love and is famous for its stunning scenic beauty. The architectural masterpiece is Agra’s top tourist attraction, but the city has many other popular destinations.

You can explore the ancient Red Fort alongside the Yamuna River and Akbar’s tomb, constructed from red sandstone. The Jama Masjid is the largest mosque in India, and Guru-ka-Tal Gurudwara are the major tourist attractions here. Thus, end your Agra day trip from Delhi with the charming sunset from the Taj Mahal.

How to reach:

Two routes are available to reach Agra from Delhi. You can get the distance of 243 km via Yamuna Expressway in just 3 hours and 44 mins. Otherwise, you can take NH 44, which will take you to just four houses and 12 minutes to the city of love.

4. Sariska National Park

Sariska National Park is your go-to place if you are an animal lover like us. Surrounded by Aravalli hills, Rajasthan’s most famous tiger reserve is the must-visit place for your one-day trip to Sariska National Park from Delhi.

Start your trip by exploring the wildlife and its green beauty with a jeep safari. You can’t miss the sight of ‘The Royal Bengal Tiger” on your journey to Sariska. During your visit, you can also see many animals, such as the Leopard, Sambhar, Nilgai, Four Horned Antelope, Langur, and Wild Boar.

How to reach:

NH 4 was the fastest route to Sariska National Park. However, due to heavy traffic, you can go through the Alwar bypass to cover the distance of 191 km in 4 hours and 29 minutes.

5. Mathura and Vrindavan

The divine city of Lord Krishna and its religious heritage always draw attention from Krishna’s devotees. The spiritual divineness flows in the breeze of Mathura, where you can still feel the presence of the Lord. The sound of his flute still flows around Shree Krishna Janmabhoomi and Dwarkadheesh temple, Govardhan hills, and Radha Kund e between the hustle and bustle of the city.

These would be your best destination for Mathura and Vrindavan day trip from Delhi by road. Stunning wall paintings and heritage architecture of Prem mandir are a must-visit for you if you are a true devotee of the ‘God of Love’ or a mythological enthusiast like us.

How to reach:

There are two routes to go on a Mathura and Vrindavan day trip from Delhi. You can cover the short distance of 182 km via the best and fastest Yamuna Expressway. Or else, you can take the NH 44 to cover the distance of 170 km in 3 hr 17 min.

6. Haridwar Rishikesh

Undoubtedly, this colourful city is another go-to destination for your one-day weekend trip. You can visit here during ‘Kumbh Mela’, where an auspicious gathering is celebrated every 12 years.

During your Haridwar Rishikesh day trip from Delhi, you can visit Har-ki-Pauri, Shantikunj, Rajaji National Park, and Mansa Devi Temple. These mesmerising sites will leave you spellbound with their rich architecture and heritage.

How to reach:

You can reach Haridwar and Rishikesh to Delhi in around 4 hours and 26 minutes via NH334, or you can go via the Panipat Expressway to cover the 263 km distance in 4 hours and 36 minutes.

These six destinations are full of historic heritage and divine beauty. You can easily escape from your daily hustle and bustle. Each destination is unique, whether you are a spiritual being or a nature lover.

Moreover, its scenic beauty will mesmerise you with its timeless elegance. Witness the serene beauty, explore local foods, and have fun with your travel partners. Enjoy the enchanting sight of the glorious sun melting away in the evening sky on this one-day road trip.
