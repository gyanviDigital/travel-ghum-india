]]> Are you also looking to escape India’s patchy and sweaty summers just like us? But planning the vacation on your own must be hectic for you. Our nation offers many refreshing summer destinations where you can enjoy your summer vacation in India. We, the Ghum India Ghum, are at your rescue with our ultimate guidance for your next summer trip.

From enjoying the pleasant Himalayan weather to the coasts of the south, we have got you covered with some popular summer destination ideas. As India’s most popular travel agency, we promise to provide you with an unforgettable journey this summer.

Let’s see the best summer destination we have chosen for your summer vacation in India:

1. Witness the phenomenal beauty of Leh-Ladakh

Leh’s timeless beauty is one of India’s best summer destinations to escape the heat waves. You can create endless, beautiful memories with your loved ones by visiting colourful monasteries like Shanti Stupa and Hemis Montessori. Enjoy your Leh trip, which is full of adventures, spirituality, and the excitement of trekking.

  • Best time to visit: The weather is pleasant from May to September, and all roads to Leh are accessible now. Thus, summer is the best time to visit the Himalayan region and enjoy your best summer trip.
  • How to reach: You can reach Kushok Bakula Rimpochee Airport in Leh from metropolitan cities like Delhi, Mumbai, and Srinagar. Or you can hire a taxi to get to your preferred destination from the airport.
  • Other than that, you can also visit Leh by state or private bus. You can take these buses from nearby cities like Manali or Srinagar.

2. Kashmir, the paradise on earth

You can visit Kashmir, the most beautiful place in India, during your summer vacation. You can enjoy the stunning scenery and pleasant weather of the ‘Paradise on Earth’. Srinagar, the capital of Kashmir, is renowned for its silver works, saffron, silk weaving, and many more. Additionally, Gulmarg, Pahalgam, and Tulip Garden are the tourist hotspots of Kashmir.

  • Best time to visit: The weather remains pleasant and calm from March to September. This is the best time to enjoy Kashmir’s iconic beauty and outdoor activities.
  • How to reach: You can reach Kashmir by taking a flight from the major cities of India to Srinagar airport, which would be the most convenient way.

Besides that, you can reach Kashmir by road trip and enjoy the scenic beauty of the hills side by side.

You can also travel by train and reach the nearest railway station of Kashmir, Jammu Tawi Railway Station.

3. The mesmerising view of Manali

The city of Manali is nestled in the beautiful Himalayan region. Its scenic beauty will take your breath away from the scorching heat waves of summer.

In addition to that, witness the splendid view of Rohtang Pass, experience the thrills of zorbing, and enjoy a bowl of hot momos with spicy red chutney amidst the alluring view.

  • Best time to visit: The best time to enjoy the beautiful views of this hill station is from March to June.

Moreover, this season offers the most fantastic hiking and camping activities to forget those patchy, sweltering summer days back home.

  • How to reach: You can get to the nearest Kullu-Manali Airport to visit Manali or take flights from Chandigarh to Manali.

Alternatively, you can travel with an unforgettable bike riding experience.

4. Gangtok, abundant in natural beauty

Gangtok’s natural beauty will take your breath away with its unique blend of spirituality and amazing scenery. You can visit famous tourist hotspots such as Kanchenjunga, Tsomgo Lake, Ban Jhakri Falls, and the Tashi viewpoint of Gangtok.

Its beautiful views, panoramic beauty, and pleasant weather make it an ideal summer destination.

  • Best time to visit: During the period of March to June, the weather is pleasant and best for sightseeing the beauty of Gangtok.
  • How to reach: You can take a flight from West Bengal’s Bagdogra airport to reach Gangtok.

Conversely, you can also travel by train via New Jalpaiguri Railway Station. The NJP station is well-connected to all the major cities in India.

Furthermore, other modes of transportation are available to reach Gangtok, such as state and private buses and shared jeeps.

5. Queen of Hills, ‘Darjeeling’

The list of summer vacations in India is incomplete without the Queen of Hills. You can visit the reclining tea gardens, dense forests, colonial mansions, and iconic toy trains of Darjeeling.

You can enjoy the favourable attractions here, including Tiger Hill, Batasia Loop, tea gardens, and the UNESCO World Heritage Site-Darjeeling Himalayan Railway. As a whole, Darjeeling is a delightful summer destination for you.

  • Best time to visit: March to June would be the most pleasant time to enjoy the scenic beauty and chilly evenings of the ‘Queen of Hills’.
  • How to reach: Several modes of transportation are available from all the metropolitan cities of India to reach Darjeeling. First, you can reach Darjeeling via Bagdogra National Airport or New Jalpaiguri Railway Station, then take a cab or taxi.

6. Ooty, the charming hill station

The charming hill station in Tamil Nadu is one of the best summer destinations in India. The alluring view of Doddabetta Peak of the Nilgiri region attracts tourists from all over the world.

Not only that, the Emerald Dam and Lake are other magnificent natural attractions in Ooty. You will fall in love even more with this hill station’s colonial architecture and pleasant climate.

  • Best time to visit: May to June is the best time to enjoy your summer vacation in India, which has the green scenic beauty of Ooty.
  • How to reach: You can take a direct flight from Delhi, Mumbai, and Chennai airports to Coimbatore International Airport.

You can also reach Ooty via its nearest Mettupalayam Railway Station. A bus or cab would take you to your Instagrammable destination, Ooty.

India’s diversity offers plenty of travel destinations for your next summer vacation in India. Escape the heat and enjoy your summer vacation with our recommended destinations’ mesmerising scenarios and amusing weather. Thus, these destinations are perfect for your next adventure or relaxation. Let’s pack your bags and get fully prepared for your picturesque vacation.
