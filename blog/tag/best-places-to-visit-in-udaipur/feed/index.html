Best Places to Visit in Udaipur

1. Fateh Sagar Lake

This is one of the four beautiful lakes whose calm water and fascinating green beauty make it one of the best places to visit in Udaipur. Moreover, nature’s endless beauty and unmatchable serenity offer you a mesmerizing reflection of its history, culture, and traditions.

Additionally, here you can participate in a variety of events and activities such as boat rides to forget your hectic travel journey and witness the green beauty and blue water in one place.

Timing: Fateh Sagar Lake is open from 8 am in the morning till 6 pm in the evening.

Ticket: Entry is free.

2. Pichola Lake

It is named after a village known as Picholi and is surrounded by palaces, islands, temples, ghats, and diverse culinary places. In fact, this place’s diverse offerings have everything for every individual tourist.

Though the main attraction that everyone admires is the view of its beautiful sunset. Alongside, a relaxing boat ride at the exact moment of sunset along with beautifully decorated boats will make you fall in love with this Places to Visit in Udaipur.

Timing: 9 am to 6 pm every day a week.

Boat Ride timing: 10 am to 5 pm.

Ticket: No entry fee is required but boat ride charges for Grownups are 400 in the morning and 700 after sunset. While children’s ticket charges are 200 and 400 consecutively.

3. Bahubali Hills

Every one of us is familiar with Udaipur’s elegant beauty and royal heritage, but do you know behind all these the place consists of some magical natural beauty such as “Bahubali hills”.

A little bit far from the main city, this nearby hill’s viewpoint will leave you amazed with its beautiful sunset views.

Best time to visit: Monsoon is the ideal season for enjoying this beautiful city.

4. Sajjangarh Monsoon Palace

Maharana Sajjan Singh built this place located on a hilltop to watch the monsoon clouds. Beside that, this place offers a beautiful view of the whole city along with its surrounding lakes, palaces, and hills. Compactly, It is an ideal place to watch the sunset and delve into a relaxing evening in Udaipur.

Timing: opening time is 9 am and closing at 5 pm.

Ticket: For Adults 250 and for Children INR 150 per head.

5. Bagore ki Haveli

You can witness the beautiful royal Rajasthani culture and the gorgeous architectural designs on this Haveli. Bagore ki Haveli is divided into several chowks where royal Rajasthani women used to perform the iconic Ghoomar dance in the middle of it in ancient days.

In particular, you can explore its culture, huge courtyards, beautiful mirror works, tranquil fountains, and decorated jharokhas if you are an art admirer like us.

Timing: 9.30 am to 5.30 pm all days of the week.

Ticket: Indians need to pay INR 60 and children’s entry fees will be INR 30.

Additionally, Foreign entry charges are 100 INR, and for children INR 50.

Additional charges are applicable on a camera of INR 50.

6. Karni Mata Temple

Don’t forget Karni Mata temple while visiting Udaipur as it is a highly religious place and what’s more is this is an abundant place for sacred rats. Goddess Durga is worshiped here as Karni Mata who holds many mystical abilities and healing powers. Alongside, devotees can spot a huge variety of rats here and explore temple rituals while enjoying the mythological depiction.

Timing: Open from 4 am to 10 pm every day a week.

Ticket: Entry is free for entering the temple.

7. Sahelio ki bari

Your Udaipur trip would be incomplete without a visit to a charming attraction named “Sahelio ki Bari”. Located on the banks of Fateh Sagar Lake, this place used to be a spot for leisure evenings of royal maidens.

Inside its colorful flower beds, marble lawns, and beautiful fountains are worth your time and perfect for attractive Instagram pictures.

Timing: Open from 9 am in the morning till 7 pm.

Ticket: The entry fee for Indians is 10 rupees but for foreigners 50 Indian rupees.

8. City palace

This 450-year-old museum palace is another major tourist attraction of Udaipur. Moreover, its unique architecture and historic lineage offer a mesmerizing birds-eye view of the city from the top of the hill.

Timing: Open all day a week 9 am to 9 pm.

Ticket: The entry fee for adults is 400 and 150 for children.

9. Gangaur Ghat

This is a wonderful place to feel the vibe of Udaipur’s vibe, especially during sunrise and sunset. It is a beautiful place for taking delightful nature pictures and it is also popular as a pre-wedding shoot destination for couples.

Timing: Every day a week from 12.00 am to 12.00 pm.

Ticket: No entry fee required.

10. Jagmandir Palace

This is a beautiful island palace where you can enjoy a relaxing boat ride from the City palace amidst the lake Pichola. Above all, this island palace offers a great sunset view for its visitors. This three-story palace also has a cafe where you can have authentic Rajasthani cuisine.

Timing: Open every day a week from 10:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m.

Ticket: There is no entry free here but the boat rides cost 450 per person.

11. Ambrai Ghat

This Ghat offers one of the best views of the surrounding lakes which is a popular destination for tourists to sit by the water and enjoy the beautiful views, especially during sunset. Additionally, the Ambari ghat is also a great place for experiencing the evening. Aartis which will be your treat to the eye.

Timing: entry timing is 6:00 am to 10:00 pm at night.

Ticket: No entry fee required.

12. Jagdish Temple

The beautiful temple in Udaipur is a perfect icon of Hindu mythology. You must come here to enjoy its special sculptures and beautiful paintings of this place and you can see many more best places to visit in Udaipur

Timing: Temple is open 5:00 am – 2:30 pm in the morning and 4:00 pm – 10:00 pm in the evening.

Ticket: Entry is free here.
