Itinerary At Glance:

This tour package takes you to one of the most beautiful and popular places in the Northern & Southern part of India. It being with Delhi arrival & Delhi city tour, sightseeing of historical sights and markets of Delhi, Next stop is Agra the Mughal city & most Iconic Taj Mahal, Then sightseeing of The beautiful Jaipur which also famously known as Pink City and it is latest entry into UNESCO official world heritage sites. After your next destination will be Kerala :-  GOD'S OWN Country where you can explore forts, beaches in Cochin, Tea plantations in Munnar, Bird Sanctuary in Kumarakom & Kerala style Houseboat in Alleppey on Back water & Cruise through the backwaters is the most memorable way to explore the beauty of the backwaters. later return to your home from Cochin. Itinerary at Glance :-
Day 01:- Arrive Delhi for Incredible India tour
On arrival at Delhi international airport, after collecting luggage and clearing immigration formalities, meet and greeted by our friendly driver cum representative outside exit gate# 5, After pickup proceed towards your pre-booked hotel in Delhi on your incredible India tours by ghum india ghum or Delhi Agra Jaipur tour with Kerala, On arrival at hotel check-in and rest of the day free to relax from jetlag. Overnight in Delhi.

Day 02:- Delhi Sightseeing tour(  Red Fort, Lotus Temple, Humayun Tomb are closed on Monday)
Today post your delicious buffet breakfast at hotel, Check-out and go for full day Delhi city tour by ghum india ghum, first go for Old Delhi city tour- by visiting Birla temple, the Raj Ghat- place is dedicated memory of Mahatma Gandhi, driving past Red fort, Then may visit Jama Masjid- India Largest Mosque are important places to visit, you may also visit Chandni Chowk, spice market etc. For a better understanding of the Old Delhi tour you may go for a Rickshaw ride tour and ride through the bazaars and streets of Old Delhi (Optional cost). Post Lunch, proceed for a city tour of New Delhi. Starts with a tour of The Qutub Minar which is a UNESCO site in Delhi and Iron pillar inside its complex. Then visit Lotus temple, which is quite similar to Sydney Opera house. It is a worship place for Bahai in Delhi. Also see Iconic India gate which is dedicated to brave Indian soldiers who lost lives during WWI and WWII, Don't forget to see huge government buildings in Delhi Like -Rashtrapati bhawan, New and Old Parliament building, Raj path. Post Delhi local tours with Ghum India Ghum drive back to the hotel. Overnight in Delhi.

Day 03:- Delhi - Agra Local Sightseeing (220 kms/04 hrs)
This morning after a healthy breakfast at the hotel  start your 3rd day of Indian Golden Triangle tour by driving to Mughal city Agra and On your arrival in Agra check-into your pre booked hotel. In afternoon our guide will take you to sightseeing tour of Agra city starts with Taj Mahal tour, the visit or time spend to the Taj Mahal will long be remembered by you, Listed among most amazing Seven Wonders of the World, the Taj Mahal is the emblem of eternal love. The Taj Mahal was built in 1653 by late Mughal emperor Shah Jahan. Built with white marble, this monument is on top of UNESCO World Heritage Sites. This epitome of love leaves the visitors speechless with its architectural magnificence. In the presence of moon light, it excels like a flawlessly carved diamond; looking as if instantly out of some magical saga and Later visit the Huge Agra fort or Red fort of Agra a UNESCO world heritage site and 2nd most famous monument of Agra, built by the great Emperor Akbar, this was main residence of Mughal rulers till 1638 buit of Red stand stones. if time permit, you may also visit Tomb of I'timād-ud-Daulah also know as Baby Taj Mahal. Post finishing place to visit in Agra tour or perfect itinerary for Agra tour or place to see in Agra or things to do in Agra or tour of important tourist attraction in Agra. Drop to Hotel, Relaxing night stay in Agra. Note:- Taj Mahal closed on Friday

Day 04:- Agra - Jaipur ( 260 kms/ Approx. 5 hrs)
After a delicious breakfast at the hotel, Start your 4th day of Golden Triangle tour India and drive to Jaipur city popularly known as the pink city India. This is the only city in the world design in such a way it symbolises the nine divisions of the universe through the nine rectangular sectors subdividing it as per Vedic architecture. The historic walled city of Jaipur in Rajasthan was established on the plain unlike other cities of this region which are situated on hilly terrains, It was founded in 1727 AD (18th Cent.) under the rule of Sawai Jai Singh II. Jaipur is known for its exemplary architectural legacy which is based on ancient Hindu, Mughal and western cultures. Pink city Jaipur made its official entry into the UNESCO World Heritage Site list recently in 2019. On arrival in Jaipur check in to your hotel stay overnight in Jaipur hotel.

Day 05:- Jaipur Local sightseeings
Post breakfast at hotel, meet your tour guide for full day Jaipur tour, start with first visiting Amber Fort (one need to reach here by 8:30-9:00 am, to avoid rush and heat) fort is situated on one end of city, Amber fort is huge is size and it has unique history, present day Amber fort was built by Raja Man Singh main attractions within Amber fort are Sheesh Mahal, Diwan-e-aam, temples and underground tunnels. Next place to see in Jaipur is Jal Mahal which is a huge palace situated in the middle of Man Sagar lake, from distance the water palace looks like its only one floor palace but in reality is four levels of this palace beneath the water. Post Lunch visit Hawa Mahal or winds palace is a architectural marvel designed by Lal Chand Ustad in 1799, The main purpose was to allow royal family ladies to see marketplace and busy streets from several jharokhas and without being seen, Then visit City palace of Jaipur which is full of architectural wonders and it is official residence of present day Royal family, it is divided into two parts one contains several courtyards along with museums and the other where present day royal family still resides. Also visit Jantar Mantar an astronomical observatory adjoining the City Palace complex, Later in evening you may visit Albert Hall and insta sensation Patrika gate, post fun filled Jaipur city tour by ghum india ghum return back to hotel and overnight in Jaipur.
 

Day 06:- Jaipur to Delhi via Udaipurwati village tour
This Morning, post yummy breakfast at the hotel in Jaipur, relax check out as per hotel check-out time and it's time to say goodbye to Jaipur with many lifelong memories of the Delhi Agra Jaipur tour, later, you will be driven back to Delhi which is Capital City of India on the way you may visit Udaipurwati is a village in Neem Ka Thana district Shekhawati region of Rajasthan, as per locals once king of Mewar was invited to Shekhwati by its ruler on seeing this beautiful landscape of rivers, stepwells , dams and hills he called this palce as Udaipur of Shekhawati. On arrival check in Delhi and rest of day free. Overnight at the hotel.

Day 07:- Delhi to Cochin by flight ( own Expense) and Kochi City tour
This morning after a yummy breakfast at Delhi hotel, you will transfer to Delhi Airport as per flight be ready for Long flight journey. On arrival at Cochin  Airport meet and Greet Ghum India Ghum Kerala tour representative and later transfer to the Hotel. After relaxing, go for sightseeing of Cochin, a city that is till today influenced by medieval Portugal, Holland and England. Your sightseeing will include visits to Chinese Fishing Nets, Fort Kochi beach, Hill Palace, Jewish Synagogue, Mattancherry Palace or Dutch Palace, Post tour of Place to visit in Kochi. Return back to the hotel for a night stay in Kochi.

Day 08:- Cochin to Munnar
​Post breakfast, Check-out and drive to Munnar, the famous and most visited hill station of Kerala. Munnar is a hill station located 1600 mts above sea level. Munnar is a famous hill station of south India and it is 1800 meters above sea level and it also houses South India's highest peak (anamudi - 2695 meters). On the way may visit Cheeyappara & Valara waterfalls , Tea Plantations. On arrival at Munnar, Check in at Hotel. Afternoon post lunch visit Rajamala (eravikulam National Park - open as per the directions by the forest authorities ), the best place to see Nilgiri Tahr (hemitragus Hilarious) - the mountain goat of South India & Tea Museum. Evening have a short visit to Munnar town. You can witness the beauty of tea estates like a green paradise. Overnight stay at Munnar Boat of South India & Tea Museum. Evening have a short visit to Munnar town. You can witness the beauty of tea estates like a green paradise. Overnight stay at Munnar.
 

Day 09:- Munnar City tour
Post tempting buffet at hotel, gear up for full day Munnar tour that will be full of fun and adventure as tourists are offered splendid sightseeing at Munnar. In Munnar sightseeing there are endless things to enjoy like Tea Museum, Tea Gardens, Mattupetty, Devikulam, Eravikulam National Park and so on. Apart from sightseeing, tourists can spend evenings by involving leisure activities in the hotel and night will be spent in the same hotel. This day will complete a tour of all places to visit in Munnar.

Day 10:- Munnar to Thekkady (110 kms / 3-4 hrs approx)
After enjoying the surroundings of Munnar, you will move to the next destination which is Thekkady. It is at 110 km distance and 3.5 hours away. At the hotel in Munnar, you will be served hot breakfast and then you can check out from there. Reaching at Thekkady, you will be accommodated in another hotel there. Post Check in formalities, you will be all set for Thekkady sightseeing in which you can enjoy Boating at Periyar, visit for Spice Garden, shopping, Kalaripayattu & Kathakali and Elephant Ride. After a long day tour of places to visit in Thekkady take a rest at a hotel and at night in Thekkady.

Day 11:- Thekkady to Alleppey (135 kms / 4 hrs approx)
After delicous breakfast at the hotel in Thekkady, check out from the hotel, Drive to next Alleppey which is our personal favroite destination in Kerala, On arrival,  Check-in  to the houseboat which is the most awaited moment of this tour. Around 1300 hrs check in at the Houseboat and have your lunch (which you can order 1 day in advance) and start your cruise through the rich and splendid view of backwater of Kerala. Just relax at the pre-booked deluxe/Premiun/Luxury houseboat for wonderful Kerala backwater tours by Ghum India Ghum. Surrounding landscape is such that It will be a unique and exclusive lifetime experience. Boats will be cruising alongside the village sides and paddy fields witnessing the core life of the Kerala villages for the whole day you may opt for Kerala Massage during your houseboat journey too. These houseboats are fully furnished with bedrooms, a kitchen and all modern amenties. Later in evening have Dinner with light romantic music and overnight stay at the houseboat.

Day 12:- Alleppey - Cochin Airport drop (60kms/1.5hrs)
This is the toughest day on your trip because you need to pack your belonging with reminiscence of your Incredible India journey, post healthy breakfast, Check-out from your houseboat in Alleppey and Our driver cum representative will pick you and drop to Kochi airport as per your return flight time, during airport transfer you can recall all your memories about India and its culture. Go back to your sweet home with a lifelong experience. Which place is known as the Golden Triangle?

Delhi Agra and Jaipur are the cities which make Indian Golden Triangle tour,  These cities are situated in three different Indian state and when you travel to these cities they make triangular shape route map, So these cities tour famously called as Indian Golden Triangle tour packages
