Itinerary at Glance :-
Day 01:- Arrival Paris (France)
On arrival at the Paris airport, meet and greet our representative and  later transferred to your pre-booked hotel on seat in coach basis. On the way enjoy the stunning view of the city. On arrival at hotel post completion of Checking formalities, rest of the day free to explore Paris by your own.

Day 02:- Paris Hop on Hop off City Tour or Places to visit in Paris tour
Post tempting breakfast at hotel, leave your hotel on your London and Paris tour package from Delhi for Paris Hop On Hop Off bus tour or Paris city tour by Hop On Hop Off bus go for the top attraction to visit in Paris starts by visiting sacred Notre Dame Cathedral is medieval Catholic cathedral church, It is regarded as one of the finest examples of French Gothic architecture, Its construction started in 1160 under supervision of Bishop Maurice de Sully and was mostly complete by 1260. Next stop the Avenue des Champs Elysees - It is famously define as world's best avenue and it is almost 2kms in length and 70 meters wide, this historic avenue running between the Place de la Concorde to the majestic Arc de Triomphe. Visit Place de la Concorde - one of the most popular public squares in Paris created in 1772, it is the largest square in the French capital. It is located at end of the Champs-Elysees and surrounded by prestigious hotels and two monumental fountains, Later visit the Arc de Triomphe - is one of the famous monument, Standing at the western end of the Champs-Élysées. At last visit most famous monument and engineering Marvel the Eiffel Tower which is most visited paid monument of the world more than 7 million tourist visit Eiffel tower every year. The Iconic Eiffel Tower in Paris is a wrought-iron tower and It is named after the Gustave Eiffel engineer whose company was behind designing and building of the tower from 1887 to 1889. You may rise-up to its 2nd floor to enjoy a breathtaking view of the surroundings of Paris. In evening you may go for Seine River Cruises in Paris to witness beauty of Paris with evening lighting. Overnight in Paris.

Day 03:- Paris to Amsterdam (Netherlands)  via Brussels (Belgium) | Brussels and Amsterdam City tour
After appetising breakfast proceed for beautiful city Amsterdam by coach. On the way, stop for tour of Brussels capital city of Belgium. Go for place to visit in Brussels by driving along the Iconic Royal Residence, Beautiful the Chinese Pavilion and Sacred St. Michaels cathedral towards the world renowned Atomium. After things to do in Brussels, Continue drive towards Amsterdam. On arrival in Amsterdam, which is also commonly known as the Venice of the north, go for Amsterdam orientation tour or places to visit in Amsterdam which includes tour of Dam Square and the Royal Palace, the mesmerising Amsterdam canal belt, the Anne Frank House, Sacred the Old Church, as well as Amsterdam local market walking tour. Post visit of important attraction of Amsterdam. In evening don't forget to go for Breathtaking Amsterdam canal cruise tour. Overnight in Amsterdam.

Day 04:- Amsterdam to  Heidelberg Post City tour of Colonge and Frankfurt (Germany)
Today morning, post healthy breakfast, Check out from hotel and proceed to visit Cologne the 4th largest city of Germany. On arrival go for places to visit in Cologne starts with Cologne Cathedral, Cologne Chocolate Museum, Brühl Castle, Historical Cologne City Hall. Post places to see in Colonge  continue your journey to Frankfurt which is a situated in central German city on bank of the river Main. Like many of the city, it was also damaged during World War II and later on rebuilt. On arrival in Frankfurt go for places to visit in Frankfurt starts with stunning skyline is visible to visitors long before they reach the city gates. Explore the old side of the city visit Romer, Goethe House, Frankfurt Cathedral. Then, explore contemporary side of city with European Banking Centre, the shopping centre, Post visit to tourist attraction of Frankfurt. Continue your journey to Heidelberg which is located on bank of the Neckar River and is majorly famous for its romantic and picturesque cityscape. Go for places to see in Heidelberg starts with Heidelberg Palace, Old Bridge, Church of the Holy Spirit, Heidelberg, Heiligenberg after visiting major tourist attraction of Heiligenberg. Check-in to hotel, Nights stay in Heiligenberg.

Day 05:- Heidelberg to Zurich (Switzerland) via Rhine falls
This morning post delightful breakfast in the hotel, Check out and continue your Europe road trip journey along the picturesque German Border, After 2.5hrs journey reach to The Rhine Falls is a wonderful waterfall located in Switzerland and It is the largest plain waterfall in Europe. The falls here are located on the High Rhine on the border between the of Schaffhausen and Zürich, you may go for boat ride here. Later continue your ride to Zurich. On arrival in go for places to see in Zurich or for Zurich city orientation tour starts with Grossmunster, Fraumunster Church, Swiss National Museum, Lindenhof hill square, do some shopping at Bahnhofstrasse, post tour important tourist attraction in Zurich, In evening spend quality time at Lake Zurich, you can may go for boating or cycling. Overnight in Zurich.

Day 06:- Zurich to Mount Titlis to Lucerne to Zurich
Today after juicy breakfast, Check out and drive to Lucerne enjoy a scenic coach ride over the Albis Pass and along the beautiful shores of Lake Lucerne. On arrival, go for short city tour of Lucerne visit the Lion Monument, Chapel Bridge and the new Concert and Congress Hall. Later post things to do in Lucerne proceed to Mount Titlis for most exciting tour on this Europe holiday package form India go through the worlds first revolving cable car. Enjoy the breathtaking sight of the glaciers deep crevasses and gigantic ice boulders from the Rotair, the lift ofrevolving gondola allows you an uninterpreted 360-degree view of the spectacular landscape. Overnight stay in Hotel in Zurich.

Day 07:​-​Zurich to Lucerne to Interlaken to Zurich
In the morning, post savory breakfast in the hotel, Check out than proceed to Interlaken on arrival go places to see in Interlaken or orientation tour of Interlaken or You also go for optional tour to visit Jungfraujoch mountain tour which is situated at an altitude of 3,466 metres above sea level and called the Top of Europe. Switzerland blessed dearly by mother nature and you will breathtaking scenery at each and every corner of Switzerland. Here you will view a 13.6 mile ice stream, an Ice Palace and views that will keep you speechless.You will even be able to get up near to a no. of the sights as your driver come guide will give you get some free time to explore the city most mesmerising monuments. Post visiting important tourist attraction Interlaken, Overnight in Zurich hotel.

Day 08:-​ ​ Zurich Airport drop
This morning post luscious breakfast at hotel, check out and drive to Zurich airport for dropping return home with lifelong unforgettable memories of Europe tour package or Paris-Amsterdam-  Switzerland tour package.
