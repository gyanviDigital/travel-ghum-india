Itinerary at Glance :-
Day 01:- Phuket Arrival
On arrival at Phuket International Airport, Post completion of immigration formalities, meet and greet our representative at waiting of airport. Later transfer to your pre-booked hotel in Phuket. Phuket city is capital town of Phuket Province of Thailand. Phuket city is in two part old and new. Old Phuket town is popular for Sino-Portuguese style of buildings and Colourful markets. Check -in to hotel and relax for few hours. In evening get ready for Phuket FantaSea show, you will be picked on time from hotel lobby, Phuket FantaSea is a cultural theme park in 60-acre where shows are based on Thailand rich culture which also includes show with elephants, animal exhibition with your appetising dinner.

Day 02:- Phuket city sightseeing tour
After yummy breakfast, get ready for exciting Phuket City Tour to explore the phantasmagoric beauties of Phuket city on your Krabi Phuket honeymoon tour package. Start your city tour by visiting clock tower and Jui Tui Shrine in old town of Phuket. Post Lunch visit Kao Khad View Tower or Panwa Viewpoint which offer 360 degree view of southern and eastern side of Phuket. Also visit Wat Chalong which is biggest and most sacred Buddhist temple in Phuket, See Big Buddha to get breathtaking panoramic view of Eastern side of Phuket, From Karon view point see Kata Noi, Kata and Karon beaches. At last visit Nai Harn Lake and Nai Harn Beach. Post places to visit in Phuket or Things to do in Phuket or important tourist attraction of Phuket, Return back to hotel. Evening free for exploring white sands beach of Phuket or you know Phuket is famous for lively nightlife you may go for Pub crawl or Bar hopping tour is Phuket. After best tourist attractions Phuket, stay overnight in Phuket.

Day 03:- Phuket to James Bond Island by Big Boat to Phuket (Excludes National Park fee)
This morning post delicious early breakfast at hotel, pick up from hotel lobby for Exciting James Bond Island day trip from Phuket, later transfers to Phang Nga Bay in Phang Nga Province by 8 am and board long tail boat. Visit Suwankuha Temple or Monkey Cave temple here visit surrounding caves and pay your respect to 15meters long reclining lord Buddha idol. then proceed for sightseeing tour in Phang Nga Bay National park in Long Tail Boat park is surrounded by Mangrove forest, Khao Majoo and huge limestone mountains. In afternoon go for seafood lunch at fishing village of Koh Panyee island, here you can experience local culture and life style. Post lunch you may go for exploring sea caves and nature by canoe at Talu Island with an experienced paddlers come your guide. Then proceed to visit Khao Phing Kan or Ko Khao Phing Kan original name of James Bond Island, famous James Bond movie The Man with the Golden Gun filmed here in 1974 since than it is popular with name James Bond Island among travellers, Post exciting James Bond island tour return back from pier to Phuket, Later transfer back to Phuket hotel, Evening free for relaxing. Night stay at Phuket.

Day 04:- Phuket to Krabi (165kms/2.5hrs)
Post appetising breakfast at hotel, checkout from hotel and drive to next exciting destination of Krabi, You will get extraordinary experience while traveling between these two beautiful cities, Krabi is one of the most visited place of Thailand and quiet poplar among tourist because it offer sheer limestone cliffs, huge dense mangrove forests, and hundred offshore islands. On arrival check-in to your pre-booked resort in Krabi. Krabi is like heaven on earth for beach lovers, rest of the day to explore Krabi city tour by own, You may visit beautiful beaches of Krabi or may go for Places to visit in Krabi such as Tiger Cave temple, Buddhist temple(Wat Kaew Korawaram), Khao Khanap Nam, etc. In Evening may go for exploring Krabi local Market and enjoy lively nightlife Krabi by going for Pub crawl or Bar hopping tour is Krabi. Post things to do in Krabi or tour of  important tourist attraction in Krabi . Night stay in Krabi.
Optional tour:- Krabi 4-Island Tour by Longtail boat four islands are  Phra Nang Cave, Tup Island, Chicken Island and Poda Island as well as tour of stunning Phra Nang Beach on the Railay peninsula. Famous for crystal clear water beaches, white powder sand beaches where you can go for Swimming, snorkelling, sunbathing.

Day 05:- Krabi to Phi Phi Island by Big Boat with Lunch (Exclude national Park fee)
This morning post tasty breakfast at hotel, great ready for adventurous Phi Phi Island Day trip from Krabi.  Pick up from hotel lobby and drop to Saphan Pla Pier, Board ferry to Phi Phi Island tour from Krabi. On arrival at Phi Phi Lay Islands go for tour of Loh Samah Bay, Pileh Cove & Viking Cave. Your 1st stop on our will be at Bamboo island which is beautiful desert island with stunning beach and amazing coral reef perfect destination for snorkelling on Krabi tour. 2nd stop is Hin Klarng island which is regarded as best places for Snorkelling on your Phi Phi Island tour because it have best ref in the open sea. 3rd stop on your tour is Monkey beach is very beautiful beach which home to several kind of marine life and many beach monkeys. 4th stop on Phi Phi Don here you will be served delicious Thai Lunch, 5th stop at Viking Cave here go for short tour of cave which have ancient wall paintings. Next stop will be 6th one at Pi Leh Bay famous for hidden sea lagoon surrounded by high limestone cliffs and last stop on the tour is 7th one at beautiful Maya bay which is one of most famous among all islands for its remarkable scenery and  Leonardo Dicaprio’s super hit movie "The beach'' was filmed here.

Day 06:- Karabi to Koh Samui (260kms/ 5hrs)
Morning post heavenly at hotel, Check out and drive to next interesting destination Koh Samui on your Ko Samui Tour With Krabi And Phuket Package. Ko Sumai is 2nd largest island of Thailand and visited million of traveller to witness its beauty. It is famous for Coconut groves, Plam Fringed beaches, dense rainforest and high end resorts. After 5 hours of road journey reach Koh Samui, on arrival check-in to hotel and relax, rest of the day free for exploring Ko Samui city tour by your own, You may visit beautiful beaches of Koh Samui or may go for Places to visit in Ko Samui. In Evening may go for exploring Ko Samui local Market, May go for authentic Thai Massage and enjoy lively nightlife Ko Samui by going for Pub crawl or Bar hopping tour is Krabi. Post things to do in Ko Samui or tour of  important tourist attraction in Ko Samui . Night stay in Ko Samui .

Day 07:- Koh Samui to Angthong National Marine Park full Day Trip
This morning post mouthwatering at hotel, Go for Angthong National Marine Park day trip from Koh Samui, Angthong is combination of 42 islands in the Gulf of Thailand and These Island are truly blessed by mother nature, These Island tour is perfect tour for adventure lovers which have beautiful hidden lagoons, Crystal clear white sand beaches, bare chalk cliffs and thick vegetation. Famous some scenes of Hollywood Movie “The Beach“ was also filmed here. The island which we are visiting on this tour are Koh Wao ( which is group of 3 islands with a shallow, sheltered bay in between, it is perfect place for snorkelling), Next one is Mae Koh ( it is an emerald- green salt lake. best for rowing along the shore), Koh Wua Talap ( here climb upto the 240mtrs to reach view point and you will be surprise by the natural beauty, which will beunfolding in front of you) and Koh Paluay. Thai Lunch will be at a fisherman village. Post island hopping tour, the speedboat will take you to the south side of the island to do kayaking with amazing cliffs and go to uninhabited islands with to see unexplored beautiful beaches. Post Angthong National Marine Park , return back the hotel. spend evening with romanic dinner with loved at Koh Samui beach restaurants. Night stay in Koh Samui.

Day 08:- Koh Samui to Bangkok (by flight/own expense) & Bangkok to Pattaya
Today morning post luscious breakfast, check out from  hotel on your Bangkok Pattaya with Krabi and Phuket. Later you will be transferred to the Samui International Airport, Koh Samui for boarding flight to Bangkok, a short 90-minutes flight. On arrival at Bangkok the Thailand capital city, Meet and greet our representative at Bangkok Airport then drive to Pattaya  which is 2nd most visited city of Thailand after Bangkok and 2.5hrs drive from Bangkok. On arrival check-in to hotel and rest of the day free to Explore Pattaya city tour by own. You may go for beautiful quiet beaches of Pattaya or Scared Temple tour of Pattaya or Pattaya's popular cabaret show and you can go for Pattaya pub crawl and also enjoy delicious Thai foods at local restaurants. Post things to do in Pattaya overnight stay in Pattaya. Optional tour Alcazar show.

Day 09:- Pattaya city tour with Coral Island tour
This morning after delightful breakfast at hotel, Later go for exciting Coral Island Tour or known locally as Koh Hae or Ko He on speed boat with lunch pick up from hotel lobby on time and transfer to ferry point from there board speedboats after 20-25mins ride reach coral point and go for guided Coral Island Tour and explore the marine life and nice coral, you may go for water sport activities such as Snorkelling, Scuba diving, Jet Skiing, etc. Post delicious lunch return back to Pattaya city. Later go for go for tour of Places to visit in Pattaya or Places to see in Pattaya, Starts with Sanctuary of Truth which is a Hindu-Buddhist temple come museum in Pattaya, next is Pattaya Floating Market which is floating market with traditional Thai style and traditional Thai crafts here on waterfront shops, then visit Big Buddha Temple or Wat Phra Yai situated on Pratamnak hill in city centre. Pattaya Viewpoint or Khao Pattaya View Point situated Pratumnak Hill is one of the best viewpoint and from here you will get breathtaking panoramic view of Pattaya city and Pattaya bay. In evening go for tour of famous beach in Pattaya starts with beautiful Jomtien Beach which is long sandy beach and its best beach in Pattaya to spend time with loved one because it is less chaotic and less crowded. at Last visit Pattaya Beach and beach road- Pattaya Beach is the most popular and most commercialised beach in Pattaya. It is a main attraction in Pattaya for marine activities, sunbathing and water sports. This 3kms long beach is full of hotels of all range, Spa centres, restaurants and souvenir shops. Evening is free for enjoying cheerful nightlife of Pattaya may visit walking street, Clubs, Bars, etc. Post best places to visit in Pattaya or best tourist attraction in Pattaya or best itinerary for Pattaya. Stay overnight in Pattaya.

Day 10:- Pattaya to Bangkok and rest of the day free.
On this day post yummy breakfast at hotel, Check out hotel with good memories of the Pattaya, drive to Bangkok which is largest city of Thailand and Bangkok is one of most visited city of world. Bangkok is famous for vibrant street life and sacred Buddhist shrines. Bangkok is political, cultural and commercial centre of Thailand. On arrival check-in to your pre-booked hotel in Bangkok. Rest of the day free for exploring Bangkok city by your own, May go for tour of local markets of Bangkok such as Ban Mai Market, Khlong Suan Market and Nakhon Nuang Khet Market. May go for dinner cruise on the Chao Phraya River which have its network of canals, flowing from important Rattanakosin royal district to opulent Grand Palace. May go for entertaining Bangkok nightlife which is another highlight of city and which attracted millions of tourists from all over the world. Post things to do in Bangkok. Stay overnight in Bangkok.

Optional tour :- Tour of Safari World in Bangkok, Thailand which have two parks Marine Park and Safari Park, founded in 1988 and operated by Safari World Public Limited. This park have total area of 480 acres and 180 acres for bird park. Best place for animal loving traveller on Thailand tour package.

Day 11:- Bangkok City tour
This morning after heavenly breakfast at hotel, go for enjoyable city of Bangkok which is not only political capital of Thailand but it is also cultural capital of Thailand and Bangkok is capital of Thailand earlier known as Siam since 1782 during Chakkri dynasty. Go for tour of places to see in Thailand or Places to see in Thailand starts with visiting The Grand Palace which is huge palace complex situated in heart of city and it is official residence of the King of Thailand since 1782, next stop is Temple of the Emerald Buddha or Wat Phra Si Rattana Satsadaram which is very important sacred Buddhist Temple complex situated inside Grand Palace in the historical centre of Bangkok, Then visit iconic Wat Arun temple or Wat Arun Ratchawararam Ratchawaramahawihan is a magnificent Buddhist temple situated on west bank of the Chao Phraya River, Name of this temple is derived from Hindu god Aruna and its very popular landmark of Thailand, Visit Wat Phra Chetuphon or Wat Po is a buddhist temple complex and commonly know for Temple of the Reclining Buddha, Pay your respect at Wat Traimit which house world largest Lord Buddha gold statue which id 10feet high and it weight more than 5ton, if time permits National Museum of Bangkok, MBK Centre, After completion of tour of Important tourist attraction of Bangkok, return back to hotel in Bangkok for night stay.

Day 12:- Bangkok Airport drop
After the tasty breakfast, pack your baggage with a promise of come back again to Amazing Thailand​ or​ Discover Thainess, Proceed to​ different Bangkok​ Airport​ as per your flight ticket ​on time to board your return journey flight with a lot of unforgettable memories of your ​Thailand h​oliday Package tour​ or ​Thailand h​oliday Package tour​ package.​
