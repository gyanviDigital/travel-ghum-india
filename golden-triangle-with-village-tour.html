Itinerary At Glance:

Ghum India Ghum, a leading tour operator in India, presents the Golden Triangle with the Village Stay package. This unique tour offers more than just a historical journey through Delhi, Agra, and Jaipur. It provides an opportunity to experience the heart of India - its villages.
India's soul resides in its villages, home to 70% of the country's population. This tour takes you deep into rural India, offering a glimpse into the culture and traditions of Indian village life. You'll stay in heritage and ethnic village homes, go on guided village walks, visit villagers' homes, and witness the rhythm of life in an Indian village.
This package is becoming increasingly popular due to its uniqueness. In addition to exploring Delhi, Agra, and Jaipur, you'll get to experience life in Tordi Garh village. As the saying goes in India, "real India is Rural India," and this tour offers you a chance to see it up close.
Tordi Garh is a village in Rajasthan that offers a wealth of sights to see. Spend a night here and immerse yourself in the authentic Indian rural experience.
As one of the best travel companies in India and a renowned tour operator in Delhi, Ghum India Ghum ensures an unforgettable travel experience. We are also recognized as a reliable Destination Management Company (DMC) for India. So pack your bags and get ready for an adventure that will leave you with memories for a lifetime.

Itinerary at Glance :-
Day 01:- Arrive Delhi for Incredible India tour
On arrival at Delhi international airport on your Delhi Agra Jaipur with Village stay , after collecting luggage and clearing immigration formalities, meet and greeted by our friendly driver cum representative outside exit gate# 5, After pickup proceed towards your pre-booked hotel in Delhi on your incredible India tours by ghum india ghum, On arrival at hotel check-in and rest of the day free to relax from jetlag. Overnight in Delhi.

Day 02:- Delhi local and Delhi to Jaipur (280kms/5hrs)
Post Delicious breakfast,Check out be ready for day long activity by 8am starts with sightseeing tour of Delhi. By visiting Old Delhi The Raj Ghat, Red fort(from outside) and Jama Masjid, Chandni Chowk, spice market most important  Later on proceed to visit the New Delhi visit India Gate, Rashtrapati Bhawan, Lotus Temple first point is The Qutub Minar, tallest stone tower in India. Post Delhi tour and lunch drive to Jaipur. Jaipur city popularly known as the pink city India. This is the only city in the world designed in such a way it symbolises the nine divisions of the universe through the nine rectangular sectors subdividing it as per Vedic architecture. The historic walled city of Jaipur in Rajasthan was established on the plain unlike other cities of this region which are situated on hilly terrains, It was founded in 1727 AD (18th Cent.) under the rule of Sawai Jai Singh II. Jaipur is known for its exemplary architectural legacy which is based on ancient Hindu, Mughal and western cultures. Pink city Jaipur made its official entry into the UNESCO World Heritage Site list recently in 2019. On arrival in Jaipur check in to your hotel stay overnight in Jaipur hotel.

Day 03:- Jaipur City tour
Post breakfast at hotel, meet your tour guide for full day Jaipur tour, start with first visiting Amber Fort (one need to reach here by 8:30-9:00 am, to avoid rush and heat) fort is situated on one end of city, Amber fort is huge is size and it has unique history, present day Amber fort was built by Raja Man Singh main attractions within Amber fort are Sheesh Mahal, Diwan-e-aam, temples and underground tunnels. Next place to see in Jaipur is Jal Mahal which is a huge palace situated in the middle of Man Sagar lake, from distance the water palace looks like its only one floor palace but in reality is four levels of this palace beneath the water. Post Lunch visit Hawa Mahal or winds palace is a architectural marvel designed by Lal Chand Ustad in 1799, The main purpose was to allow royal family ladies to see marketplace and busy streets from several jharokhas and without being seen, Then visit City palace of Jaipur which is full of architectural wonders and it is official residence of present day Royal family, it is divided into two parts one contains several courtyards along with museums and the other where present day royal family still resides. Also visit Jantar Mantar an astronomical observatory adjoining the City Palace complex, Later in evening you may visit Albert Hall and insta sensation Patrika gate, post fun filled Jaipur city tour by ghum india ghum return back to hotel and overnight in Jaipur.

Day 04:- Jaipur to Tordi Garh
Post healthy breakfast proceed to Tordi Garh. On arrival check in Hotel relax for sometime at the hotel and have lunch (Option to have traditional lunch at a heritage and ethnic village home), Later Take an excursion to Todaraisingh Fort and Hada Rani ki Bawri (Step Wells) - Which was nominated for World Heritage Site. Later Back to Palace and Later in the afternoon may take a jeep ride(optional) visit beautiful countryside and Tordi Sagar Dam and to spot wild animals and migratory birds. Visit organic farms to know how different grains and vegetables are grown. Later on Take a visit to Tordisagar Lake and Tordi sand dunes by camel cart(optional). In Evening Have hot tea and cookies at the sand dunes. Back to Tordi Palace. Enjoy Bonn Fire on top of the terrace overlooking starry skies. Dinner and overnight in Tordi.

Day 05: Tordi Garh to Agra ​and Agra Local (Agra fort & Mehtab Bagh )
Sip your morning tea and then take a guided hill trek to Tordi Garh, walking through the village lanes and then taking an easy climb up the hill. Back to the Palace and have a relaxing breakfast. Post breakfast it is time to say goodbye to Tordi Garh palace, check out from the hotel with a lot of lifelong memories. Later on proceed to Mughal city Agra and check into your hotel. After relaxing in a hotel for few hours visit famous UNESCO heritage sites first the Massive Agra fort, built by the great Emperor Akbar entire fort is constructed by Red SandStone, if time permits you can visit the famous Mehtab Bagh situated just backside of Taj Mahal and you can click many breathtaking pictures of Taj Mahal from here. Overnight in Agra.

Day 06:- Sunrise Taj Mahal  tour and Drop to Delhi
Today early morning with sunrise visit the most beautiful monument of the world Taj Mahal-the symbol of love, built by Mughal King Shah Jahan in memory of his late wife Mumtaz Mahal. Its UNESCO site in Agra and One of the most visited monuments in India, Taj Mahal Looks more beautiful in sunrise, Sunrise tours are subject to weather conditions. Later you will be dropped at New Delhi International Airport. This is the toughest day on your trip because you need to pack your belonging with reminiscence of your Incredible India journey, post healthy breakfast, Check-out from your hotel in Agra, Our driver cum representative will pick you and drop to Mumbai airport as per your return flight time, during airport transfer you can recall all your memories about India and its culture. Go back to your sweet home with a lifelong experience. Don't forget to write your review  for Ghum India Ghum which is a leading tour operator in India, travel agency in Delhi, travel agents in Delhi, Tour Operator for India, On Incredible India tour with Indian village stay which let you know the Indian culture by spending time in the village.
Note :- Taj Mahal closed on Friday & Book night return flight from Delhi.
