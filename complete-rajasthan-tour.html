Itinerary:

01
Day 01:- Arrival in Jaipur and local exploration

Kickstart your Complete Rajasthan Tour with a warm welcome upon your arrival at Jaipur Airport or Railway Station. We'll drive straight into the city's history and beauty as we start our neighborhood tour. Ascend the enduring Amber Castle, a masterpiece of the 16th century, and don't pass over the hazard for an elephant journey (at your own cost). Marvel at the serene splendor of Jal Mahal and the architectural wonder of Hawa Mahal. After a lovely lunch, delve deeper into Jaipur's heritage with visits to the majestic town palace and the astronomical wonders of Jantar Mantar. Finish your day with a stroll through the famed Albert Corridor Museum. rest and rejuvenate at your Jaipur resort.

02
Day 02:-Full Day Visit of Jaipur

After a luxurious breakfast, set out to discover more of Jaipur's treasures. Undertaking to Nahargarh and Jaigarh forts, providing panoramic perspectives of the town and its surroundings. Respect the grandeur of Jaigarh fortress, overlooking the picturesque Maota Lake. Spend the evening leisurely, perhaps indulging in some shopping close to your hotel, and come back to your hotel for a restful overnight stay in Jaipur.

03
Day 03:-Drive to Bikaner and Visit

Start your third day of the Complete Rajasthan Tour with a hearty breakfast before departing for Bikaner. Upon arrival, immerse yourself in the rich history and colorful lifestyle of this desert town. Explore the imposing Junagarh Castle and the exquisite Lallgarh Palace. Don't pass over the possibility to go to the charming Deshnok Rat Temple and the renowned Camel Breeding Farm. After an afternoon full of exploration, retire to your hotel in Bikaner for the night.

04
Day 04:-Drive to Jaisalmer

Begin your day with a nutritious breakfast before embarking on the adventure to Jaisalmer. Marvel at the ever-changing landscapes of the wilderness en route to this golden metropolis. Discover the majestic Jaisalmer fortress, walk through the problematic havelis, and immerse yourself in the timeless allure of the wasteland. Loosen up and unwind at your inn in Jaisalmer.

 

05
Day 05:- Full Day Visit of Jaisalmer and Camp Stay

Savor a scrumptious buffet breakfast before delving deeper into the enthralling town of Jaisalmer. Explore the majestic Jaisalmer fort and wonder at the tricky craftsmanship of the Jain temples. Later, assignment into the captivating Khuri Sand Dunes for a camel safari and witness a breathtaking sunset. Spend the night underneath the celebrities at a desolate tract camp in Jaisalmer.

06
Day 06:-Drive to Jodhpur

After a tempting breakfast, leave for the majestic metropolis of Jodhpur, additionally referred to as the Blue City. Discover the bold Mehrangarh citadel, walk through the colorful markets, and indulge in the neighborhood delicacies. Retire to your lodge in Jodhpur for a non-violent night's relaxation.

07
Day 07:- Full Day Visit of Jodhpur & Drive to Udaipur Via Ranakpur

Embark on a captivating adventure via Jodhpur's rich records and traditions. Explore the majestic Mehrangarh fort, the elegant Umaid Bhawan Palace, and the serene Jaswant Thada. Later, adventure to the romantic city of Udaipur, stopping en route to go to the superb Jain temples of Ranakpur. Take a look at your resort in Udaipur and unwind for the night.

08
Day 08: Full-Day Visit to Udaipur

After a tasty breakfast, embark on a captivating excursion to Udaipur. Discover the majestic metropolis palace, be surprised by the elaborate craftsmanship of the folk art museum, and pay homage at the ancient Pratap Memorial. Conclude your day with a visit to the scenic Moti Magri, overlooking Fateh Sagar Lake. Return to your hotel for a relaxing night in Udaipur.

09
Day 09: Departure from Udaipur/Jaipur Airport

Take pleasure in a wholesome breakfast before bidding farewell to the captivating land of the Complete Rajasthan Tour. Our representatives will transfer you to Udaipur or Jaipur Airport/Railway Station for your onward adventure. Depart away with fond memories of your Complete Rajasthan tour.Return home with priceless experiences that will last a lifetime.
