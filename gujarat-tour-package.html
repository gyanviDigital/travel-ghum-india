Itinerary At Glance:

This is one of the best tour packages for Gujarat if you are interested in seeing Gujarat in detail. This Gujarat holiday package starts with Arrival in Ahmedabad and gives you a chance to explore cities like Ahmedabad and Jamnagar​ which are modern cities with a traditional touch. Visit Dwarka, Bet Dwarka  and Somnath which are Centre of spirituality in Gujarat, Also give you chance to see wildlife tourism in Gujarat by visiting Gir National Park is famous for huge Asiatic lions and also this national park is called as Sasan Gir, winter time is best time to visit Gir National Park. These Gujarat packages also take you to the Statue of Unity in Kevadia which is the most visited place in Gujarat from its day of Opening. Gir National park tour packages are one of the best Gujarat tour packages.

Itinerary at Glance :-
Day 01:- Ahmedabad arrival and Ahmedabad sightseeing tour
On arrival at Ahmedabad railway station/ Airport, meet and greet our friendly representative outside the exit gate, Then transfer to your pre-booked hotel in Ahmedabad which is the biggest city in Gujarat and homes to several UNESCO heritage sites in the state. Ahmedabad city is situated on the bank of River Sabarmati.  Ahmedabad is a UNESCO's World Heritage City list of 2017 and it has 28 monuments register under ASI, After relaxing for few hours go for city tour starts with visiting Gandhi Ashram or Sabarmati Ashram situated on bank of Sabarmati river it is place from where Mahatma Gandhi set out on his Dandi March and which is now converted into a huge museum, Hutheesing Jain Temple built in 1846 which is a sacred Jain temple which hold very important place in Jainism, this temple is dedicated to Lord Dharmanatha, the 15th Jain Tirthankar, Next stop is Sidi Saiyad Mosque, then Spend quality time at Kankaria Lake you may go for boating at biggest lake in Ahmedabad, Also See Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel museum dedicated to Iron man of India​. Have your lunch at a local restaurant, You can suggest it to your driver or you can take suggestions from the driver or us, Post Lunch visit Adalaj Stepwell or Rudabai Stepwell is a stepwell situated in the village of Adalaj near Gandhinagar, it famous for outstanding example of Indian architecture work. End your Ahmedabad city tour by visiting the impressive Akshardham temple which is Hindu temple built by carved stone structures and here you may go for an amazing light and sound show at sunset.  Later post Ahmedabad tourism returned back to the hotel for overnight stay.

Day 02:- Ahmedabad to Statue of unity to  Ahmedabad (200kms/3.5hrs each way)
This morning post yummy breakfast at the hotel, go for most exciting tour on your Gujarat holiday package, by driving to Statue of unity day tour from Ahmedabad or Ahmedabad to statue of unity tours, which is 3 to 4 hrs drive from Ahmedabad, It is just 100 kms southeast of the city of Vadodara and 150 kms from Surat. Statue of unity is situated facing beautiful Sardar Sarovar Dam on the river Narmada in Kevadiya, The Statue of Unity dedicated to the great Indian freedom fighter, Iron man of India and first Deputy prime minister of India ' Vallabhbhai Jhaverbhai Patel' also popularly called as Sardar Patel. Mr Patel dedication to national integration by uniting 562 princely states in the newly independent India due to which he earned the title of "Iron Man of India". Statue of unity is the world's tallest statue with a height of 597 fts or 182 metres, it was designed by famous Indian sculptor Ram V. Sutar. Places to visit on Statue of unity day such as Museum, Valley of Flower, Sardar Sarovar Dam Viewpoints if you stay there till evening may see light and sound shows. If time permits you may go for places to visit in Vadodara like Laxmi Vilas Palace, Sur Sagar lake and Sayaji Baug. Later, returned back to Vadodara for a night stay.
Note :- Statue of unity closed on Monday

Day 03:- Ahmedabad to Jamnagar (310kms/5.5hrs)
This morning post appetising breakfast, Check out from the hotel and drive to Jamnagar also historically known as Nawanagar. On arrival check in to the hotel. Later, proceed to important places to visit in Jamnagar like the most beautiful Lakhota Lake and 19th century building Lakhota palace and Museum, Kotha Bastion, also visit the historical Darbargadh Palace whose foundation stone was laid by Jam Rawal​. Have your lunch at a local restaurant, You can suggest it to your driver or you can take suggestions from the driver or us,, Post lunch, Visit other important monuments such as Pratap Vilas Palace, Khijada Temple, RatanBai Masjid, Cremation Park. In the evening you may visit Bala Hanuman Temple situated next to Ranmal Lake which is famous for its non-stop Ramdhun since 1956 and it holds a record in Guinness Book of World Records. Overnight stay at the Hotel.

Day 04:- Jamnagar to Dwarka (140kms/2.5hrs)
Today morning after a juicy breakfast, check out from the hotel and travel to Dwarka. Dwarka is one of the important Dham out of sacred Chardham as per Hindu religion and it is one of the Sapta Puri which means seven most ancient religious cities in the country. On arrival check-in to the hotel. Later go for important places to visit in Dwarka start with visiting Dwarkadhish Temple Hindu temple, situated on Gomti creek and dedicated to Lord Krishna. Also take a holy dip in Gomti river in Dwarka​. Have your lunch at a local restaurant, You can suggest it to your driver or you can take suggestions from the driver or us, later post lunch visit Nageshwar Jyotirling which is one of the 12 Jyotirlinga of Lord Shiva in India, Gopi Talav place where Gopis united with Lord Krishna, Gopi Tava is on the way to Bet Dwarka by ferry (Own expense), Bet Dwarka is situated on Island it scared Hindu pilgrimage site it is believed that it was original home of lord Krishna during his stay at Dwarka and on way back do visit Rukmani Temple, return back to Dwarka overnight at hotel.

Day 05:- Dwarka to Somnath Via Porbandar (231kms/5hrs)
After tempting breakfast, check out from the hotel and proceed to Somnath via Porbandar which is 2hrs drive from Dwarka. Porbandar city is popular for the birthplace of Mahatma Gandhi and another personality Sudama who was a friend of Lord Krishna. It is also popularly known as Sudamapuri. At Porbandar, the best places to visit in Porbandar starts with visiting Kirti Mandir, the memorial house and the place where Mahatma Gandhi was born, now converted into a small museum dedicated to him. Also visit Sudama Temple. It is the only temple dedicated to Sudama in the world. Post Lunch visit Chowpatty beach in Porbandar, Huzoor Palace is built by Rana Natwarsinhji, and also go for a temple tour in Porbandar visit Shri Hari Mandir and Gayatri Mandir. Later drive continue your drive to Somnath, On Arrival in Somnath, check-in to the hotel and after relaxing for few hours proceed for places to visit in Somnath starts with darshan at Somnath Temple- it is the first among the twelve Jyotirlinga shrines of the Lord Shiva and Bhalka Tirth is the place where Lord Krishna was hit by an arrow shot by a hunter named Jara. Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 06:- Somnath to Gir National Park ( 70kms/2.5hrs)
Today morning after a juicy breakfast, check out from the hotel and drive to another exciting destination Gir National Park and Wildlife Sanctuary, famously known as Sasan Gir which is  next to Talala Gir in Gujarat. On arrival check-in to the hotel and later in the afternoon go for Jungle Safari in Sasan Gir (Own Expense), this national park was set up in 1965 with the aim of protecting Asiatic lions, This national park is a naturally developed and well-managed Ecotourism in India. Gir National park hosts almost 523 Asiatic lions and its number is increasing every year as per Asiatic Lion Census. On your ​Gir national park tour package you get chance to see many animal apart from iconic Asiatic Lions because it homes more than 300 species of Birds, around 36 species of reptiles and almost 38 species of Mammals, You may also visit Gir Interpretation Zone at Devaliya, Crocodile breeding farm and Gir Information Centre. Post gir jungle jeep safari tour package returns back to hotel rest of time free to enjoy hotel amenities. Night stay at Gir National park resort.

Day 07:- Sasan Gir to Diu (100kms/2.5hrs)
Enjoy morning you may again go for a Jeep Safari to Gir national park (at your own cost) . Where you can look for famous Asiatic Lions and get the pleasure of watching other wildlife inside Gir Wildlife Sanctuary. Later Post yummy breakfast at the hotel, Check-out and drive to a very famous beach destination in India which is Diu located on western part of India . On arrival on your Diu tour packages, check-in to the hotel and later go for the best places to visit in Diu, starting with Diu Fort- most famous and most visited monuments in Diu, Built in 15355. St. Paul Church-is an ancient church constructed in 1610, Diu Museum- iconic St Thomas Church converted into Museum to preserve thing belonging from vast Diu history, Gangeshwar Temple - it is an ancient lord Shiva temple and Noida Caves is heaven for nature lovers and photographers because view from here is breathtaking. Later​, Have your lunch at a local restaurant, You can suggest it to your driver or you can take suggestions from the driver or us, ​Post lunch visit Nagoa Beach and go for water sports facilities here. In the evening You can spend quality time going to the beach on the Diu tour. Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 08:- Diu to Ahmedabad (370kms/8hrs)
After a healthy breakfast at the hotel, pack your bag and baggages with wonderful memories of Cultural Tour Of Gujarat or Ahmedabad-Bhuj-Rann of Kutch tour package, check out from hotel and drive to Ahmedabad you will be dropped to the Railway Station or Airport, return home with lifelong memories of the Gujarat trip.
