Overview

This is one of the most popular Indian Golden triangle tour packages. In this tour package you will see two different Landscapes of India, Delhi Agra Jaipur is situated on plains whereas Kashmir is a hilly area. Kashmir is mostly covered with snow during winter time and famously called as Paradise on Earth and it holds a very special place in every Indian heart.  This tour package is quite famous amongst people coming from Malaysia, Singapore, Arab countries and many more. Kashmir is abundantly blessed by mother nature, you will get a chance for Shikara ride on incredibly beautiful Dal Lake. Gulmarg is Kashmir is famous for Skiing in India, it hosts several domestic and international winter sports. You will fall in love with Kashmir the moment you land in Srinagar which is the starting and End point of Kashmir part of this Indian Golden Triangle with Kashmir. Itinerary At Glance:

Itinerary at Glance:-
Day 01:- Arrive Delhi on Incredible India Journey
Upon your arrival at Delhi airport after clearing the immigration, you collect the baggage from the luggage belt, then after passing through the custom area and you will find our representative will be holding a paging board with your name outside Exit gate#5 and Post meet and greet, drop to your pre booked hotel in Delhi in spacious car. Overnight in Delhi.

Day  02:- City Tour of Delhi (Note :- Many Delhi monuments closed on Monday)
Today after a delicious breakfast at the hotel, enjoy a full day exploring Delhi, pacing through this amazing city which is historic as  well as modern, like 2 sides of the same coin! Enjoy a morning sightseeing tour of Old Delhi, which includes a visit to Jama Masjid, the largest mosque in India, a rickshaw ride (additional cost) through narrow alleys and lanes in Chandni Chowk, one of the oldest and largest trading markets of the country and drive past Red Fort, the place that marked the announcement of India’s freedom from the British Raj.  Later in the afternoon, discover the modern city of New Delhi, visiting the Qutub Minar, a fine example of Indo-Islamic architecture and the world’s tallest brick minaret and Gurudwara Bangla Sahib, the largest Sikh temple of Delhi, with a gold dome, still in active worship. Drive past Presidents House, Parliament House and other Government Secretariat Buildings, the most imposing features of Lutyens Delhi and take a photo stop at the iconic war memorial arch, the India Gate. Overnight in Delhi.

Day 03:- Delhi to Agra & Agra Local (Agra Fort & Mehtab Bagh) (220kms/04hrs)
Today post breakfast at hotel, Check-out and Later drive to Mughal city Agra and check into your hotel. Later, visit the Huge Agra fort or Red fort of Agra a UNESCO world heritage site and 2nd most famous monument of Agra, built by the great Emperor Akbar, this was the main residence of Mughal rulers till 1638 buit of Red stand stones & Later visit our personal favourite Mehtab Bagh- which is located just backside of incredibly beautiful Taj Mahal from here you can take many pictures with stunning background views and pictures of Taj Mahal. Post tour return back to hotel and Overnight in Agra.

Day 04:- Sunrise Taj Mahal Visit and Agra to Jaipur Via Monkey Temple (260kms/5hrs) 
Today wake, up early in the morning to go for the Sunrise Taj Mahal tour, the visit or time spent at the Taj Mahal will long be remembered by you, Listed among most amazing Seven Wonders of the World, the Taj Mahal is the emblem of eternal love. The Taj Mahal was constructed around 1653 by late Mughal emperor Shah Jahan. Built with white marble, this monument is on top of UNESCO World Heritage Sites. This epitome of love leaves the visitors speechless with its architectural magnificence. In the presence of moon light, it excels like a flawlessly carved diamond; looking as if instantly out of some magical saga and  Post tour return back to the hotel have your delicious breakfast and post some relaxing check-out and drive to Jaipur - the fabled “Pink city” of the desert named after Jai Singh, the former maharaja of Jaipur. This is the one and only city in the world which symbolizes the nine divisions of the universe through the nine rectangular sectors subdividing it. The palaces and forts of the yesteryears, which were witnesses to the royal processions and splendorous are now living monuments en-route visit Galtaji Temple or Monkey temple this temple is famous for its natural springs and most sacred one is Galtaji Spring or Galta Kund which get dry ever, this temple not only attract devotees but also friendly Monkeys. Galtaji temple is 10kms before Jaipur on Agra- Jaipur highway. Arrive Jaipur in late evening & transfer to hotel. Night stay in Jaipur. Note:- Taj Mahal Closed on Friday
 
Day 05:- Jaipur City Tour
This morning after yummy breakfast at hotel, go for full day city tour start by visiting Iconic the Amber fort which was built in the 16th century This fort overlooks amazing Maota Lake which is act as main source of water for massive The Amber palace, Amber fort architecture is best example for rich Hindu and mughal architecture. here you may enjoy an elephant ride(own Expense) at Amber fort. Later visit the amazing Jal Mahal of Jaipur which is built in the middle of the Man Sagar Lake, and also drive past the Hawa Mahal-a Palace or palace of winds which is the best place in Jaipur for stunning social media pictures. In the afternoon you will visit the city palace and museum, and walk to the adjacent Jantar Mantar, an astronomical observatory. Stay overnight at a hotel in Jaipur.

Day 06:- Jaipur to Delhi stay (280kms,5-6hrs)
Today post mouth-watering breakfast, you will be transferred back to Delhi is of great historical significance as an important commercial, transport, and cultural hub, as well as the political centre of India, On arrival in Delhi, Check-in to the hotel overnight in Delhi.

Day 07:- Delhi to Srinagar(by flight own expense)|  Arrival at Srinagar
Today post breakfast, Check-out and drop at Delhi airport to board a flight to Srinagar. On your arrival at the Srinagar Airport, meet and greet our representative, drive to Srinagar city by crossing through Abdullah bridge which connects two major banks of the Jhelum river. Check-in to the hotel and later go for a Srinagar city tour by visiting iconic Mughal gardens of Nishat garden, Shalimar garden, Chashmashahi, Parimahal, In the evening enjoy an hour Shikara ride on ​iconic Dal Lake. Overnight in Srinagar.

Day 08:- Srinagar - Sonamarg ​- Srinagar (80kms/2-3 hrs each way)
Today after yummy breakfast, go for day trip to Sonamarg from Srinagar, Sonamarg is 80kms from Srinagar drive via the Sindh Valley which presents yet another spectacular facet of the countryside in Kashmir. Sonamarg is situated at an altitude of approx. 2730 m, Sonamarg (‘the meadow of gold') has an amazing backdrop of snowy mountains against a cerulean sky. Optionally visit Thajiwas glacier, a major local attraction during the summer months, evening back return to Srinagar, overnight stay at Hotel.

Day 09:- Srinagar - Pahalgam​- Srinagar (90kms/ 2-3hrs each way)
After mouthwatering breakfast, go for a day trip to Pahalgam, Pahalgam is 90kms from Srinagar, drive to Pahalgam via Pampore, Avantipura and the village of Bijbehara which remains famous as the bread basket of Kashmir. On route to Pahalgam we exit national highway 1A at Khanabal midpoint then drive through Anantnag which is the second largest city of Kashmir region. From here onwards the road turns into scenic views as we travel parallel on the Lidder river flowing from the opposite direction. you can enjoy trip to Chandanwari and Aru as well as horse riding (at your own cost), evening back return to Srinagar Overnight stay at Hotel
Note (Only Ponies Are Used for Baisaran Valley)

Day 10: Srinagar - Gulmarg ​- Srinagar (51kms/ 2hrs each way)
After appetizing breakfast, go for another exciting day tour from Srinagar to Gulmarg and Gulmarg is 51kms from Srinagar. On the way you will get chance to visit amazing Tangmarg town and from here its 14kms scenic view drive to Gulmarg. On Arrival at Gulmarg Start a short tour, by boarding the Gondola cable car system (the 08 minutes ropeway, additional pay and advance booking required). Descend back to Gulmarg after an hour and later indulge in some horse-riding and Skiing activities because Gulmarg is a famous Ski destination in India for professional Sking competitions. Evening back return to Srinagar Transfer to Houseboat. This Evening enjoys a romantic Shikara ride on the Dal Lake Overnight Stay Houseboat. Note (Shikara Ride We Will Provide for 1 Hour if you want enjoy more ride in dal lake that is chargeable

Day 11: Srinagar to Delhi (by flight /own Expense) and Delhi Departure
Today, post breakfast, you will be transferred to Srinagar Airport to board on flight for your onward journey.  Return home with sweet memories of Beauty of Taj &  Historical monuments of Delhi & Jaipur. Don’t forget to fill the feedback form available with the driver, it helps us in improving our services and if you like our services don’t forget to write a review on Trip Advisor.
