Itinerary At Glance:

Commence an unforgettable journey through the heart of North India with Ghum India Ghum, a leading tour operator in India. This tour package offers a comprehensive exploration of Delhi, Agra, Jaipur, and Amritsar, some of the most beautiful places in the region. Your journey begins in Delhi, where you'll delve into the city's rich history and vibrant markets. Next, you'll visit Agra, home to the iconic Taj Mahal, a symbol of Mughal grandeur. The adventure continues in Jaipur, also known as the Pink City for its distinctive color. Here, you'll experience the city's royal heritage and colorful culture.
Finally, you'll head to Amritsar, a city founded in 1577 by the fourth Sikh guru, Ram Das. As the holiest city of Sikhism, Amritsar is home to the spectacular Golden Temple, the holiest gurdwara (religious complex) of the Sikh religion.
With Ghum India Ghum, one of the best travel companies in India and a reputable DMC for India, your tour will be a memorable one. So why wait? Commence your adventure today!

Itinerary at Glance :-
Day 01:- Arrive Delhi​ on incredible India tour with Golden Temple
On arrival at Delhi international airport, after collecting luggage and clearing immigration formalities, meet and greeted by our friendly driver cum representative outside exit gate# 5, After pickup proceed towards your pre-booked hotel in Delhi on your incredible India tours by ghum india ghum, On arrival at hotel check-in and rest of the day free to relax from jetlag. Overnight in Delhi.
 

Day 02:- Delhi Sightseeing tour( Red Fort, Lotus Temple, Humayun Tomb are closed on Monday)
Today post your delicious buffet breakfast at hotel, Check-out and go for full day Delhi city tour by ghum india ghum, first go for Old Delhi city tour- by visiting Birla temple, the Raj Ghat- place is dedicated memory of Mahatma Gandhi, driving past Red fort, Then may visit Jama Masjid- India Largest Mosque are important places to visit, you may also visit Chandni Chowk, spice market etc. For a better understanding of the Old Delhi tour you may go for a Rickshaw ride tour and ride through the bazaars and streets of Old Delhi (Optional cost). Post Lunch, proceed for a city tour of New Delhi. Starts with a tour of The Qutub Minar which is a UNESCO site in Delhi and Iron pillar inside its complex. Then visit Lotus temple, which is quite similar to Sydney Opera house. It is a worship place for Bahai in Delhi. Also see Iconic India gate which is dedicated to brave Indian soldiers who lost lives during WWI and WWII, Don't forget to see huge government buildings in Delhi Like -Rashtrapati bhawan, New and Old Parliament building, Raj path. Post Delhi local tours with Ghum India Ghum drive back to the hotel. Overnight in Delhi.
 
Day 03:- Delhi to Agra and Agra Sightseeing tour (220 kms/04 hrs)
Post delicious breakfast at your hotel, start 3rd  day of Golden Triangle India tours by driving to Mughal city Agra and on arrival check into your hotel. This afternoon visit the Agra fort or Red fort of Agra, built by the great Emperor Akbar, this was main residence of Mughal rulers till 1638 built of Red stand stones and post lunch visit world famous Taj Mahal-the symbol of love, built by Emperor Shah Jahan which was commissioned in 1632 to house the remains of his beloved wife Mumtaz Mahal. You may also visit the Tomb of I'timād-ud-Daulah also known as Baby Taj Mahal. Post finishing place to visit in Agra tour or perfect itinerary for Agra tour or place to see in Agra or things to do in Agra or tour of important tourist attractions in Agra. Drop to Hotel, Relaxing night stay in Agra. Note:- Taj Mahal closed on Friday

Day 04:- Agra to Jaipur ( 260 kms/ Approx. 5 hrs)
After a delicious breakfast at the hotel, Start your 4th day of Golden Triangle tour India and drive to Jaipur city popularly known as the pink city India. This is the only city in the world design in such a way it symbolises the nine divisions of the universe through the nine rectangular sectors subdividing it as per Vedic architecture. The historic walled city of Jaipur in Rajasthan was established on the plain unlike other cities of this region which are situated on hilly terrains, It was founded in 1727 AD (18th Cent.) under the rule of Sawai Jai Singh II. Jaipur is known for its exemplary architectural legacy which is based on ancient Hindu, Mughal and western cultures. Pink city Jaipur made its official entry into the UNESCO World Heritage Site list recently in 2019. On arrival in Jaipur check in to your hotel stay overnight in Jaipur hotel.
 
Day 05:- Jaipur Local sightseeings
Post breakfast at hotel, meet your tour guide for full day Jaipur tour, start with first visiting Amber Fort (one need to reach here by 8:30-9:00 am, to avoid rush and heat) fort is situated on one end of city, Amber fort is huge is size and it has unique history, present day Amber fort was built by Raja Man Singh main attractions within Amber fort are Sheesh Mahal, Diwan-e-aam, temples and underground tunnels. Next place to see in Jaipur is Jal Mahal which is a huge palace situated in the middle of Man Sagar lake, from distance the water palace looks like its only one floor palace but in reality is four levels of this palace beneath the water. Post Lunch visit Hawa Mahal or winds palace is a architectural marvel designed by Lal Chand Ustad in 1799, The main purpose was to allow royal family ladies to see marketplace and busy streets from several jharokhas and without being seen, Then visit City palace of Jaipur which is full of architectural wonders and it is official residence of present day Royal family, it is divided into two parts one contains several courtyards along with museums and the other where present day royal family still resides. Also visit Jantar Mantar an astronomical observatory adjoining the City Palace complex, Later in evening you may visit Albert Hall and insta sensation Patrika gate, post fun filled Jaipur city tour by ghum india ghum return back to hotel and overnight in Jaipur.
 
Day 06:- Jaipur to Delhi and Delhi to Amritsar(by train)| Amritsar stay
Today post delicious breakfast  at​ the hotel​ in Jaipur ,​Check-out and later you will be transferred back to Delhi and transfer to Delhi railway station to board the train to Amritsar which is Located on the north-western border of India. It is a 400 years old city, founded by the 4th guru of the Sikhs, Guru Ramdas, from its inception Amritsar has been the most important seat of the Sikh religion and for its culture. It is famous for the Golden Temple, Amritsar means -'Pool of Nectar' name derived from the holy pond around the temple. On arrival at Amritsar station meet and greet our representative at Platform and later transfer to your pre-booked hotel. Overnight at a hotel in Amritsar.

Day 07:- Amritsar sightseeing tour
Today after a delicious breakfast, you will visit the famous Golden Temple, Also known as the Darbar Sahib, which is the holiest​ worship place for Shikhs and the most important pilgrimage site of Sikhism. It is located in the city of Amritsar, Punjab and  Later visits Jallianwala Bagh in Amritsar, the iconic and heritage site where hundreds of innocent Indian men, women and children were massacred in broad daylight by British General Michael Dyer on April 13,1919. This incident totally changed the course of Indian independence struggle. A memorial and garden have been built here as a national monument to remember the sacrifice of the people.​You can go for a Heritage walk in Old Amritsar town which is the area surrounding Golden Temple, these places are recently renovated by the government. Post Lunch​, today, enjoy an exciting beating retreat ceremony between India and Pakistan at Atari - Wagah border (One needs to reach there by 1500 hrs). Post finishing Places to visit in Amritsar or best itinerary for Amritsar tour or places to see in Amritsar, Stay overnight in Amritsar.

Day 08:- Amritsar airport drop or Amritsar- Delhi (by Train) & Transfer to Delhi International Airport
This is the toughest day on your trip because you need to pack your belonging with reminiscence of your Incredible India journey, post healthy breakfast, Check-out from your hotel in ​A​mritsar and Our driver cum representative will pick you and drop to ​A​mritsar airport/Railway station as per your return flight time, during airport transfer you can recall all your memories about India and its culture. Go back to your sweet home with a lifelong experience.
