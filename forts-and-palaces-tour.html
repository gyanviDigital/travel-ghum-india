Itinerary at Glance :-
Day 01:- Arrival Delhi
On arrival at Delhi, meet and greet our friendly representative outside exit gate no :- 05 on India Gandhi International airport, Later transfer to your pre-booked hotel in luxury and comfortable car, Rest of the free for leisure. Night stay in Delhi.

Day 02:- Delhi City tour
After delicious breakfast gear up for your visit full day Old Delhi and New Delhi city tour : go for a full day sightseeing tour of Delhi  starting with old Delhi tour by visiting - Birla Temple, The Red Fort, Jama Masjid, The silver street of Chandni Chowk, Raj Ghat. Later post Lunch go for New Delhi tour starts with The India Gate, The Rashtrapati Bhavan - President's House, Parliament house, The tomb of Emperor Humayun, Qutab Minar and The Bahai Temple(Lotus Temple). Overnight in Delhi.

Day 03:- Delhi - Agra City tour ( By Surface)
Post delicious breakfast at your hotel, start 3rd day of colorful Rajasthan tours by driving to Mughal city Agra(220 kms/04 hrs) and on arrival check into your hotel. This afternoon visit the Agra fort or Red fort of Agra, built by the great Emperor Akbar, this was main residence of Mughal rulers till 1638 built of Red stand stones and post lunch visit world famous Taj Mahal-the symbol of love, built by Emperor Shah Jahan which was commissioned in 1632 to house the remains of his beloved wife Mumtaz Mahal. You may also visit the Tomb of I'timād-ud-Daulah also known as Baby Taj Mahal . Post tour by evening return to your hotel for overnight stay. Note:- Taj Mahal closed on Friday.

Day 04:- Agra - Samode village tour
Post healthy buffet breakfast at hotel, Check out and start your 4th Day of Forts and Palaces Tour of Rajasthan by driving to Samode by road (275 kms / 05 hrs) en-route may visit famously Fatehpur Sikri. On your arrival, check-into the Samode Palace. Post Lunch go for a Village tour of Samode which is famous for its fort and Haveli. You may enjoy a camel cart ride during your Samode village walk tour. Our guides will take you through historical lanes of this Village giving you detail about the royal  past of forts and palaces of Samode with various glorifying stories. On the way to the village walk tour interact with the local people of Samode, who are famous for their warm and affectionate nature. Later in the evening you enjoy Rajasthani cultural show inside the palace which includes folk music and dance followed by dinner at Samode Palace. Local folk bands and musicians with their unique and very rare musical instruments provide one of the best colourful evenings one has on the tour to Rajasthan. Night stay in Samode Palace.

Day 05:- Samode ​to​ Jaipur
After a healthy breakfast at the hotel, Start your 5th day of Rajasthan tour package and drive to Jaipur popularly known as pink city India. Jaipur is the only city in the world symbolising the nine divisions of the universe through the nine rectangular sectors subdividing it. The historic walled city of Jaipur in Rajasthan, which was founded in 1727 AD under the rule of Sawai Jai Singh II. Jaipur is popular for its exemplary architectural legacy and vibrant culture, it made its official entry into the UNESCO World Heritage Site list recently in 2019. On arrival in Jaipur by late evening & transfer to hotel. Night stay in Jaipur.

Day 06:- Jaipur City tour
This morning post breakfast, then go for City tour by visiting the Amber fort, built in the 16th century famous for its artistic style, enjoy an elephant ride(own Expense) at Amber, later visit Jal Mahal which is situated in middle of Man Sagar Lake. Drive past the Hawa Mahal-a Palace of winds,This architectural feature allowed cool air from the Venturi effect to pass through, thus making the whole area more pleasant during the high temperatures in summer. In the afternoon post Lunch you will visit the city palace and museum the magnificent pillars, lattice or jali work, carved marble interiors and inlaid ornamentations make the palace beautiful in appearance and a treat to watch for the visitors, walk to adjacent Jantar Mantar an astronomical observatory, you may also visit famous Albert hall. Stay overnight at a hotel in Jaipur. Places to visit in Jaipur

Day 07:- Jaipur - Khimsar
After yummy breakfast drive to Khimsar. Post Arrival in Khimsar , check in at the hotel. Post lunch go for a tour of Khimsar or Places to see in Khimsar. Khimsar is steeped in history and was founded 2500 years ago by the Jain Saint Mahaveer. This place is the starting point of the great Indian Desert.  The  Castle over here was founded in the 15th Century by Prince Karamsi- son of Jodhpur and very close to other places of interest like Osian and Nagaur. Very well​ preserved​ Khimsar Fort has a lot to offer to the visitors with all modern available amenities- 50 fully luxuriously furnished rooms, theme dinners, a variety of continental and Indian meals as well as traditional Rajasthani food are available. Most mesmerising is evening jeep safari to sand dunes for a sunset view. Night stay in Khimsar.

Day 08:- Khimsar  village tour
Post tempting breakfast at the hotel. Full Day is free to enjoy the fort and its surroundings. Late afternoon, go for Black buck safari in India (Khimsar)  followed by evening come picnic tea on the nearby Sand Dunes in Khimsar. Overnight at the Khimsar Fort.

Day 09:- Khimsar - Jodhpur city tour
Post tempting breakfast at Khimsar fort, checkout with many lifelong memories of life in village of Rajasthan (India) and drive to 3rd most famous city of Rajasthan which is blue city of Jodhpur, situated on the edge of the great Thar Desert. This afternoon visit the former traditional seat of the Marwar rulers, the imposing Mehrangarh Fortress which perches in stately grandeur above the city. Night stay in Jodhpur.

Day 10:- Jodhpur - Udaipur Via Ranakpur
After tasty breakfast drive to Rajasthan 2nd biggest city and also known as one of the romantic cities of the world Udaipur on the way visit Ranakpur Temple are acclaimed world-wide for their intricate and superb architectural style. These temples come under one of the five major pilgrimages of the Jains. Post Ranakpur proceeds towards Udaipur. Arrival Udaipur check inn Hotel. Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 11:- Udaipur city tour
This Morning post yummy buffet breakfast at hotel, Later start your 11th day or fort and palaces tour of Rajasthan by guided city tour of Udaipur city or Places to visit in Udaipur, this city is built in 1559 A.D. by Maharaja Udai Singh and has been described as the most romantic spot on the subcontinent of India by Col. James Todd. First visit the City Palace museum, The City Palace, described as one of the largest palaces in the world, can be approached through the Bari Pol or the Great Gat with the royal drums and then onto the Tripolia Gate. Tripolia is a triple gate made of marble arches. Between these two gates are eight carved toranas or archways under which the Maharanas were weighed against gold and silver which was later distributed to the poor People. Famous entertainment games like elephant fights took place in the court beyond the Tripolia Gate. Bhartiya Lok Kala Mandir also called as Folk art museum the amusing collection displayed by this Indian folk arts museum includes folk dresses, ornaments, puppets, masks, dolls, folk musical instruments, folk deities and paintings. Also visit the very respected Pratap Memorial (Historic site that is dedicated to the gallant Maharana Pratap). A stop at the Moti Magri or Pearl Hill, overlooking the Fateh Sagar Lake is the memorial of the Rajput hero Maharana Pratap with a bronze statue of the Maharana astride his favorite horse"Chetak".Night stay in Udaipur.

Day 12:- Udaipur ​to​ Pushkar
After yummy breakfast, depart for Pushkar by road (300 kms / 06 hrs). On arrival, check into the hotel. Pushkar is located to the northwest of Ajmer.Pushkar is surrounded by hillocks on three sides. Places to visit in Pushkar is Brahma temple (said to be one of the few temples in the world dedicated to this deity), holy Pushkar lake, Man Mahal and Savitri temple by cable car to enjoy aerial view. Also visit Cattle fair ground where the world famous Pushkar Cattle Fair is held every year. You may go on a camel or tractor ride to the nearby village and desert, After completing the sightseeing tour of Pushkar then you will be transferred to your hotel in Jaipur. Overnight stay at a hotel in Jaipur.    

Day 13:- Pushkar ​to​ Neemrana
After mouthwatering breakfast at hotel, depart for Neemrana by road (280 kms / 06 hrs). Neemrana, located in between Jaipur and Delhi, it will be the last stop of your Forts and Palaces tour. On your arrival in Neemrana, check-into the Neemrana Fort Palace. Overnight at the Neemrana Fort Palace

Day 14:- Neemrana to Delhi
After tasty breakfast at the hotel, go for a guided tour of Neemrana fort later on check out and depart for Delhi by road (120 kms / 02 hrs). On your arrival in Delhi, check-into your prebooked hotel, Rest of the day free for leisure.  Overnight in Delhi.

Day 15:- Depart Delhi
Post Healthy breakfast at hotel, pack your baggage with a promise of coming back again. Proceed to Delhi Airport/Railway station on time to board your flight/train with a lot of sweet memories of your holidays.


After delicious breakfast gear up for your visit full day Old Delhi and New Delhi city tour : go for a full day sightseeing tour of Delhi  starting with old Delhi tour by visiting - Birla Temple, The Red Fort, Jama Masjid, The silver street of Chandni Chowk, Raj Ghat. Later post Lunch go for New Delhi tour starts with The India Gate, The Rashtrapati Bhavan - President's House, Parliament house, The tomb of Emperor Humayun, Qutab Minar and The Bahai Temple(Lotus Temple). Overnight in Delhi.
