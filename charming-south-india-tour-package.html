Itinerary At Glance:

This is one of the best tour packages customized by Ghum India Ghum experts Team. This includes a tour of almost all the major hill stations of south India such as Ooty , Coonor, Kodaikanal and Munnar along with Thekkady and Alleppey. This special tour of Hill Station of south India takes you through three major south Indian states, starts with arrival in Bangalore which is IT capital of India, then visits the royal city of Mysore which is famous for Legacy of Royals. From here you will enter Tamil Nadu and you will be touring Ooty and Kodaikanal tour packages which are two famous hill stations of Tamil Nadu. From Kodaikanal you will enter Kerala state which attracts most tourists in Southern India. In Kerala you will be visiting Munnar, Thekkady and Alleppey. In these 11 Days and 10 Nights you will cover Mysore, Ooty, Coonoor Kodaikanal, Munnar,Thekkady and  Alleppey and visit three important states of South India. These are all the places  which will refresh your mind with its beautiful and Natural Scenario. Ooty Kodaikanal with Kerala tour package. This is best South India hill stations with Kerala tour itinerary and travel packages which can be customized too

Itinerary at Glance:-
Day 01:- Bangalore Arrival and Bangalore local
On arrival at Bangalore airport or Railway station, Meet and greet our representative, Outside exit gates and then transfer to your pre-booked hotel. Bangalore is capital city of Karnataka state whose tagline is One state Many Worlds, Bangalore is hub for information technology in India, After relaxing and having lunch at some local restaurants like Kailash Prabat or other ones. Later go, for Bangalore half day sightseeing tour starts with Lalbagh Botanical Gardens, Vidhana Soudha, Cubbon Park, ISKCON Temple and Palace Ground, evening free for shopping and later returning back to the hotel for Overnight stay.

Day 02:- Bangalore to Mysore (150kms/3hrs)|Mysore local
After tempting breakfast, check out the hotel and drive to Mysore which is 2nd most Populous city of Karnataka state and also considered as Culture capital of Karnataka, Mysore was capital during the Wodeyar dynasty. On arrival Check-in to hotel and post relaxing for a few hours and go for best places to visit in Mysore start with Chamundi hills named after goddess Chamundeshwari Temple and Chamundi hills are magnificent viewpoints which offer breathtaking wide angle view of Mysore city, then visit Mysore Zoo and St. Philomena's Church.After having lunch at local restaurants, Post Lunch visit world famous Mysore Palace situated in the city centre and facing Chamundi hills eastward, It is an official residence of Wadiyar dynasty and in the evening visit huge Brindavan Gardens which is spread across 60 acres of land and is famous for its musical Color Fountain. Post Mysore tourism tour returns back to the hotel for overnight stay.

Day 03:- Mysore to Ooty (125kms/3hrs)
This Morning after a tasty breakfast, check out the hotel and drive to Ooty which is the most visited hill station of South India and is situated in the Nilgiri hills, also called The Blue Mountains. Ooty greets its visitors by Beautiful lakes with fresh water, Mighty mountains, thick dense forests, sprawling grasslands, eucalyptus trees and Never ending miles of tea gardens. On the way, you may visit Bandipur National Park or Mudumalai National Park and Pykara Waterfall is a famous picnic spot. On arrival at Ooty, check in to the hotel, after having lunch at local restaurants and rest of time free for leisure and shopping. Overnight stay in Ooty.

Day 04:- Ooty to Coonoor to Ooty
Post yummy buffet breakfast at hotel, Get ready for fun-filled sightseeing tour of Ooty and Coonoor start by visiting 20kms from Ooty, go for famous Coonoor Tea Gardens, Lambs rock view point, Dolphin Nose is hidden gem it provide breathtaking view of beautiful Catherine Falls, Post Important places to visit in Coonoor. After having lunch at local restaurants, Proceed to Ooty (by toy train - optional) or Car. On arrival go for Ooty Sightseeing tour star with Iconic Doddabetta Peak is highest point in Ooty and 2637 meters above sea level, Then visit Botanical Gardens, a famous tourist spot of Ooty, also visit Ooty lake and Rose Garden. Post Important tourist places in Ooty which is also known as Queen of hill stations, return back to hotel, Overnight in Ooty.

Day 05:- Ooty to Kodaikanal (260kms/7hrs)
After temping breakfast, check out from Hotel and drive to Kodaikanal from Ooty. Kodaikanal is the 2nd most popular hill station of Tamil Nadu after Ooty. Kodaikanal is a Tamil name which means "The Gift of the Forest". On the way, have lunch at local restaurants and On arrival in Kodaikanal, check-in to the hotel. Later visit the Bryant Park which is famous botanical garden of Kodaikanal spread in 20.5 acres and Build in 1908, it homes almost 325 varieties of trees and 740 varieties of Roses and Kodaikanal Lake is manmade lake build 1863, it is star shape and spread in 60 acres, Kodaikanal is main tourist attraction of Kodaikanal here you can hire Rowboats, Pedalos, you can spend your evening walking around Kodaikanal lake which is blessed with mother nature in and around. Overnight stay at Hotel in Kodaikanal.  

Day 06:- Kodaikanal sightseeing tour
This morning post delicious breakfast, Get ready for the most exciting day of the tour, go for best Sightseeing and Tourist Attractions in Kodaikanal. Later, see the Coaker's walk is named after Lt Coaker who constructed it in 1872. It is a 1km Pedestrian paved path that goes around steep slopes of the southern part of Kodaikanal, Green Valley view is the viewpoint of Kodaikanal. From here you will get a breathtaking panoramic view of Kodaikanal city. After having lunch at local restaurants visit Pillar rocks in Kodaikanal from the view point from where you can see mountains and three giant shaped rock pillars which are 400fts high, If timer permits You may also visit Chettiar park, Shenbaganur museum and Silver Cascade waterfall. Post places to visit in Kodaikanal tour return back to the hotel for overnight stay at Kodaikanal.

Day 07:-Kodaikanal to Munnar (170 kms / 5hrs approx)
After yummy breakfast at the hotel, drive to Munnar, the famous and most visited hill station of Kerala. Munnar is a hill station located 1600 mts above sea level. Once it used to be a summer resort during British administration time in South India it still holds its colonial charm and is famous for its never ending tea plantations, most of which are privately owned. With its huge estates, sparkling waterfalls, rolling hills, picture postcard hamlets and undulating valleys, Munnar is an ideal destination for nature lovers. On arrival check in your pre-booked hotel, Rest of the Day is free to relax and to walk around. Stay overnight in Munnar.

Day 08:- Munnar Local tour (130kms/4hrs)
After yummy breakfast at hotel proceed for fun filled Munnar sightseeing tours, start with morning visit the Mattupetty dam, the Kundala Lake and Echo point. Please note boating facilities are available at Kundala Lake and  Mattupetty Dam which are optional tours. Later visit a tea factory and tea museum. Afternoon post lunch visit Eravikulam National Park which is also popularly known as Rajamala, the natural habitat of Nilgiri Tahr. Nilgiri Tahr can be observed very closely here. Evening free for shopping or to see Kathakali and Martial art shows. Post places to see in Munnar or Best Itinerary for Munnar tour, nights stay at hotel.

Day 09:- Munnar to Thekkady (110 kms / 3.5 hrs approx)
After breakfast a healthy buffet breakfast at hotel, Check out and drive to Thekkady . Thekkady is famous around the world for its wildlife sanctuary and it is also known as Periyar. On arrival check in at the hotel, Later in the afternoon post lunch, proceed for a boat cruise on Periyar Lake (additional cost). Here you will be able to see a variety of wildlife animals, which include elephants, monkeys, deer, wild boar etc.  In the evening you may take an Elephant ride at Periyar wildlife and rest of the day is at leisure. Stay overnight at a hotel in Thekkady.

Day 10:- Thekkady to Alleppey (135 kms / 4 hrs)
After delicious breakfast at the hotel in Thekkady, check out from the hotel, Drive to next Alleppey which is our personal favorite destination in Kerala, Alleppey famously called as "Venice of India". On arrival at Alleppey, Check-in  to the houseboat which is the most awaited moment of this tour. Around 1300 hrs check in at the Houseboat and have your lunch (which you can order 1 day in advance) and start your cruise through the rich and splendid view of the backwater of Kerala. Just relax at the pre-booked deluxe/Premium/Luxury houseboat for wonderful Kerala backwater tours by Ghum India Ghum. Surrounding landscape is such that It will be a unique and exclusive lifetime experience. Boats will be cruising alongside the village sides and paddy fields witnessing the core life of the Kerala villages for the whole day. You may opt for Kerala Massage during your houseboat journey too. These houseboats are fully furnished with bedrooms, a kitchen and all modern amenities. Later in the evening have Dinner with light romantic music and overnight stay at the houseboat.

Day 11:- Alleppey - Cochin dropping (55km/1.5hrs)
This is the toughest day on your trip because you need to pack your belonging with reminiscence of your Incredible India journey, post healthy breakfast, Check-out from your Houseboat in Alleppey and Our driver cum representative will pick you and drop to Kochi airport as per your return flight time, during airport transfer you can recall all your memories about India and its culture. Go back to your sweet home with a lifelong experience.
