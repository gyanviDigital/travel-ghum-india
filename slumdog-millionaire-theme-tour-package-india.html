Itinerary At Glance:

This tour package is perfectly suited for those who are looking for understanding life in slums of Mumbai. ​For this tour Mumbai acts as an arrival city and Delhi as departure city. On your 8 Days cultural Essence of Golden Triangle with Slumdog millionaire Tour,  It starts with a tour of Mumbai old name is Bombay is famous not only for high rising buildings but also for its slums which you will find into the length and breadth of the City. The​ Slumdog Millionaire is​ story of an orphaned street ​boy​ named Jamal Malik who was main character of the movie ​how he ​overcom​es with his​ hardship to win a fortune​ of Million dollars​ on a game show and ​later ​walk away with his childhood ​love of life ​sweetheart ​most of the movie was filmed in ​Mumbai and few parts in Agra and other parts of India. Main selling point of this tour package is the Dharavi slum or Dharavi slum tour, Dharavi slum is considered as the largest slum in Asia and it is the world's most densely populated area. Dharavi Slums are spread over 2.2sq kms and home to around 1million people. Many parts of the Oscar winning movie "Slumdog millionaire" was filmed there. The main message from Oscar winning movie Slumdog millionaire was that bookish knowledge is not everything in life one can learn from day to day practical experiences too. Guided Dharavi slum tour takes you to a length and breadth inside Dharavi slum and shows you different shades of Life, Dharavi has its own informal economy you will see a lot of households involved in small industries of leather, Pottery and Textiles annual turnover of Dharavi stand more than USD 1 Billion. On your Private Slumdog Millionaire Tour in Dharavi with Slum visit you will be amazed to see so many happy faces there even living in such tough conditions. After Private Slumdog Millionaire Tour based in Mumbai, Next is the tour of three very Famous Historical cities of North India :- Delhi, Agra and Jaipur.

The Slumdog millionaire movie featured actor Dev Patel as Jamal Malik  main character of the movie and was directed by British director Danny Boyle. The plot of the movie was almost based on the book Q & A by Vikas Swarup.
Three locations where you can visit on Slumdog millionaire theme tour on your journey to Incredible India:  
In Mumbai – Is the city in film where most of the story takes place.
In Agra – Explore Taj Mahal and other parts because Jamal Malik acted as a tour guide at the Taj Mahal for a brief period of time in the movie.
At Iconic Chhatrapati Shivaji Terminus train station – the place where the end credits, dance sequence on hit song Jai Ho was filmed.

Then you will visit the city palace and​ well preserved​ museum inside it, In 1727 King of Jaipur Maharaja Swai Jai Singh 2 moved from Amber to City Palace, walking to adjacent Jantar Mantar​ which is another Unesco site in Jaipur​,​ ​Jantar Mantar​ is​ an astronomical observatory. Post completion of places to visit in Jaipur or Best itinerary for Jaipur tour go for relaxing night in Jaipur hotel.

Day 06:- Jaipur ​to ​​ Agra

On arrival in Agra and check into a hotel.
