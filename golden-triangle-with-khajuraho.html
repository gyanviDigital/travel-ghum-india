Itinerary At Glance:

Set off on an enriching journey through the heart of India with Ghum India Ghum, a leading tour operator in India. This tour package, known as the Golden Triangle Tour with Khajuraho and Varanasi, offers a comprehensive exploration of Delhi, Agra, Jaipur, Orchha, Khajuraho, and Varanasi.

Your journey begins in Delhi, where you'll delve into the city's rich history and vibrant markets. Next, you'll visit Jaipur, also known as the Pink City for its distinctive color. Recently added to the UNESCO heritage sites list, Jaipur is a city steeped in royal heritage and colorful culture.

The adventure continues in Agra, home to the iconic Taj Mahal, a symbol of Mughal grandeur. From there, you'll travel by train to Orchha, established by Rudra Pratap Singh sometime after 1501. Here you'll visit the Fort of Orchha, the Chaturbhuj Temple and Raj Mandir built by 'Madhukar Shah'.

Next on your itinerary is Khajuraho, a UNESCO World Heritage Site known for its group of Jain and Hindu temples. Built between 950 and 1050 by the Chandela dynasty, these monuments are a testament to India's rich cultural history.

Finally, you'll head to Varanasi or Kashi, "The Cultural Capital of India". This ancient city is one of the most important spiritual destinations in the country.

With Ghum India Ghum, one of the best travel companies in India and a reputable DMC for India, your Golden Triangle tour with Khajuraho and Varanasi will be an unforgettable experience. So why wait? Set off on your adventure today!

Itinerary at Glance :-
Day 01:- Arrive Delhi for Incredible India Golden Triangle tour with Khajuraho and Varanasi
On arrival at Delhi international airport on your Indian Golden Triangle with Khajuraho and Varanasi tour, after collecting luggage and clearing immigration formalities, meet and greeted by our friendly driver cum representative outside exit gate# 5, After pickup proceed towards your pre-booked hotel in Delhi on your incredible India tours by ghum india ghum, On arrival at hotel check-in and rest of the day free to relax from jetlag. Overnight in Delhi.

Day 02:- Delhi Sightseeing tour( Red Fort, Lotus Temple, Humayun Tomb are closed on Monday)
Today post your delicious buffet breakfast at hotel, Check-out and go for full day Delhi city tour by ghum india ghum, first go for Old Delhi city tour- by visiting Birla temple, the Raj Ghat- place is dedicated memory of Mahatma Gandhi, driving past Red fort, Then may visit Jama Masjid- India Largest Mosque are important places to visit, you may also visit Chandni Chowk, spice market etc. For a better understanding of the Old Delhi tour you may go for a Rickshaw ride tour and ride through the bazaars and streets of Old Delhi (Optional cost). Post Lunch, proceed for a city tour of New Delhi. Starts with a tour of The Qutub Minar which is a UNESCO site in Delhi and Iron pillar inside its complex. Then visit Lotus temple, which is quite similar to Sydney Opera house. It is a worship place for Bahai in Delhi. Also see Iconic India gate which is dedicated to brave Indian soldiers who lost lives during WWI and WWII, Don't forget to see huge government buildings in Delhi Like -Rashtrapati bhawan, New and Old Parliament building, Raj path. Post Delhi local tours with Ghum India Ghum drive back to the hotel. Overnight in Delhi.

Day 03:- Delhi to Jaipur (290kms-6hrs)
After a healthy breakfast at the hotel, Start your 3rd day of Golden Triangle tour India and drive to Jaipur city popularly known as the pink city India. This is the only city in the world design in such a way it symbolises the nine divisions of the universe through the nine rectangular sectors subdividing it as per Vedic architecture. The historic walled city of Jaipur in Rajasthan was established on the plain unlike other cities of this region which are situated on hilly terrains, It was founded in 1727 AD (18th Cent.) under the rule of Sawai Jai Singh II. Jaipur is known for its exemplary architectural legacy which is based on ancient Hindu, Mughal and western cultures. Pink city Jaipur made its official entry into the UNESCO World Heritage Site list recently in 2019. On arrival in Jaipur check in to your hotel stay overnight in Jaipur hotel.

Day 04:- Jaipur Local sightseeings
Post breakfast at hotel, meet your tour guide for full day Jaipur tour, start with first visiting Amber Fort (one need to reach here by 8:30-9:00 am, to avoid rush and heat) fort is situated on one end of city, Amber fort is huge is size and it has unique history, present day Amber fort was built by Raja Man Singh main attractions within Amber fort are Sheesh Mahal, Diwan-e-aam, temples and underground tunnels. Next place to see in Jaipur is Jal Mahal which is a huge palace situated in the middle of Man Sagar lake, from distance the water palace looks like its only one floor palace but in reality is four levels of this palace beneath the water. Post Lunch visit Hawa Mahal or winds palace is a architectural marvel designed by Lal Chand Ustad in 1799, The main purpose was to allow royal family ladies to see marketplace and busy streets from several jharokhas and without being seen, Then visit City palace of Jaipur which is full of architectural wonders and it is official residence of present day Royal family, it is divided into two parts one contains several courtyards along with museums and the other where present day royal family still resides. Also visit Jantar Mantar an astronomical observatory adjoining the City Palace complex, Later in evening you may visit Albert Hall and insta sensation Patrika gate, post fun filled Jaipur city tour by ghum india ghum return back to hotel and overnight in Jaipur.

Day 05:-Jaipur to Agra Local Sightseeing via Fatehpur Sikri (220 kms/05 hrs)
This morning after a lavish breakfast at hotel, check out from hotel and drive to Mughal City Agra. On the way don't forget to visit Fatehpur Sikri- a perfectly preserved red sandstone ghost town which was the estranged capital of Mughal King Akbar and was built in 1569 and deserted after failure of its water system. On arrival in Agra meet and greet your tour guide, check into a hotel and Later go for an Agra city tour or things to do in Agra by visiting Agra Fort-the rusty and huge red-sandstone fort of Agra stands on the banks of the river Yamuna. This fort is divided into many parts, the main place is Diwan-I-Am (hall for public audience), a colonnaded hall made of red-sandstone with a throne alcove of inlaid marble at the back, the Emperor heard public petitions. Second important part is the Diwan-i-Khas (hall for private audience)which is built of marble pavilions with floral inlays lend an ethereal ambience, the Emperor sat on his gem-studded Peacock Throne and used to met foreign ambassadors and rulers of friendly kingdoms. If timer permits do visit Baby Taj Mahal and Mehtab Bagh in Agra. From Mehtab Bagh you can take many stunning pics with the beautiful Taj Mahal in its background. Post Places to visit in Agra or Places to See in Agra or tour of important tourist attractions in Agra. Overnight in Agra.

Day 06:- Sunrise Taj mahal tour, Agra- Jhansi (by train), Jhansi- Orchha- Khajuraho
Freshen up and get ready to explore the beauty of Taj Mahal at sunrise(subject to weather condition). The Taj Mahal is listed among the Seven Wonders of the World and is the emblem of eternal love. It was built in 1653 by Mughal emperor Shah Jahan. Constructed of white marble, this monument is among the UNESCO World Heritage Sites. This epitome of love leaves the visitors awe-struck with its architectural magnificence. Later return to the hotel to enjoy your delicious breakfast with memories of the Taj Mahal. Later Check out and transfer to the railway station to board the fast air-conditioned train to Jhansi. After arrival in Jhansi railway station meet greet our friendly representative and drive to Orchha and later transfer to your pre-booked hotel at Orchha. Orchha is an architectural heritage town in Niwari district of Madhya Pradesh, Orchha historical town was founded by Rudra Pratap Singh in the 16th century from Bundela dynasty. Later Bir Singh Deo, king of Orchha who was a great builder during his time he spread Bundeli architectural styles not only in Orchha but to several parts of North India. Later post relaxing for a few hours, go for a place to see in Orchha. Orchha is the well-kept medieval township, Orchha was built in the 16th century. Orchha Sightseeing include tour of Rajmahal with its beautiful murals, also visit the Laxmi Narayan Temple and the Jehangir Mahal built in the 17th century, to commemorate the visit of the emperor Jehangir.Post tour of Places to visit in Orchha or Things to do in Orchha,  drive to Khajuraho, On arrival check-in to hotel and overnight in Khajuraho. Note:- Taj Mahal closed on Friday

Day 07:- Khajuraho City Tour
Post yummy breakfast at hotel go for places to see in Khajuraho or Khajuraho guided tour start with visiting Khajuraho and its temples, Explore Khajuraho Eastern and Western Group of Temple, these temples built during the the Chandela dynasty rule from 950-1050. Out of 85 temples only 22 temples from original temples group survived till now, All these temples are magnificent examples of Hindu architectures. Though all the temples are worth a visit, you visit the main attractions of Khajuraho which are the Parsvanath Temple, the Adinath temple. Post tour of places to visit in Khajuraho or best itinerary for Khajuraho tour or things to do in Khajuraho Khajuraho go for The Khajuraho Group of Monuments tour which is of Hindu temples and Jain temples, these temples UNESCO World Heritage Site since 1986. Stay overnight in Khajuraho.

Another exciting additional tour for this day on direct payment post tourist place to visit in Khajuraho you may opt for Evening Panna Jungle safari tour from Khajuraho, Panna is only 1hr drive from Khajuraho. Panna National Park is 22nd National park of India and it was declared in 1992. Evening Jungle Safari in Panna will let you experience the dense ambience of forest and give you a chance to encounter the wildest ranges of mammals, reptiles, several species of migratory birds, flora and fauna. Other than this, you will even be able to get the chance to spot the tiger species at very close quarters, Later you will return back to the hotel for Night stay in Khajuraho.

Day 08:- Khajuraho to Varanasi by flight ( own expense) & Kashi Local
Post mouthwatering buffet breakfast at hotel, you will be transferred to Khajuraho Airport to board the flight to Varanasi. On arrival at Oldest city of world Varanasi meet and greet our representative, Later do hotel check in and after a few hours of relaxation. This afternoon post lunch you will visit to Annapurna Devi, Manas Temple, Vishwanath Temple, Sankat Mochan, Durga Kund, Vishalakshi Devi and Birla Temple – Banaras Hindu University, later visit famous ghats of Varanasi :- Harishchandra, Manikarnaka also known as burning ghat (It is believed that a dead human's soul finds salvation, when cremated here), Dashashwamedh Ghat,etc. In the evening, watch the world famous Ganga Aarti. After completion of memorable places to visit in Varanasi tour, enjoy your overnight stay in Kashi.

Day 09:- Varanasi (Boat ride)- Sarnath- Varanasi
In early morning one can choose to take subah-e-banaras(Sunrise of Varanasi) boat trip to enjoy the sunrise and also see historical ghats of Varanasi. In the boat trip one can understand the tradition, culture of Kashi. Later in the afternoon, Sarnath is the place where Lord Buddha had preached his sermon/dharma to his first five disciples after attaining enlightenment. In Sarnath visit Dhamek Stupa - most visited and known as the most important Buddhist shrine in the world. Later, visit Sarnath museum, Thai temple, Main temple.Overnight in Varanasi.

Day 10:- Varanasi to Delhi by Flight(own expense) & Transfer to International Airport
This is the toughest day on your trip because you need to pack your belonging with reminiscence of your Incredible India journey, post healthy breakfast, Check-out from your hotel in Varanasi and Our driver cum representative will pick you and drop to Varanasi airport as per your return flight time, during airport transfer you can recall all your memories about India and its culture. Go back to your sweet home with a lifelong experience. Faq's:

Which place is known as the Golden Triangle?

Delhi Agra and Jaipur are the cities which make Indian Golden Triangle tour,  These cities are situated in three different Indian state and when you travel to these cities they make triangular shape route map, So these cities tour famously called as Indian Golden Triangle tour packages

Which are places included in with Golden Triangle with Khajuraho and Varanasi?

Delhi, Agra and Jaipur are the cities which make the Indian Golden Triangle tour, apart From these 3 places this package also gives you a chance to visit ​Khajuraho and Varanasi. Khajuraho is situated in Madhya Pradesh and home to one of the biggest groups of medieval time Jain and Hindu temples.  Varanasi is spiritual place in India and one of the most sacred places for Hindus, Varanasi is famous for its narrow lanes, huge numbers of ancient temple with unique history associated with each of them, Boat ride on River ganges, walking tour of Ghats in Varanasi and world famous Ganga aarti at sunset on dashashwamedh ghat.
