Itinerary At Glance:

This is one of the most famous tour package for Europe trip, it covers tour of two amzing countries Switzerland and France. This tour starts with arrival in Zurich which is regarded as gateway to paradise on Earth Switzerland, tour of Interlaken city with day trip to iconic Mount Jungfraujoch which is situated on the top of Europe, at 3454 meters. Lucerne city tour with full day Mt Titlis tour with breathtaking views. Second part of the tour includes tour of Paris capital city of France. As per Thomas Jefferson- A walk about Paris will provide lessons in history, beauty, and in the point of life.  This Swiss Paris tour package includes Full Day paris city tour with evening boat cruise and full day tour of Disneyland Studio. This is very famous honeymoon tour package for Europe from India, This tour package perfectly suited for Couples.

Itinerary at Glance :-
Day 01:- Arrival at Zurich International Airport and train journey Zurich to Interlaken (120kms/1hrs 40 mins)
On Arrival at Zurich International Airport, Meet and greet our local representative at airport waiting area, than board train from Airport to Interlaken, Explore beauty of Switzerland on the way to Interlaken, On arrival check-in to hotel and rest of the free for rest. Overnight at Interlaken hotel.

Day 02:- Interlaken Sightseeing full day Tour
This morning post yummy breakfast at the hotel, go for a full day trip to iconic Mount Jungfraujoch situated on the top of Europe, at 3454 meters. Europe's highest altitude railway station at Jungfraujoch is the major highlight of each and every visit to Switzerland. From Here you can enjoy breathtaking aerial views of the beautiful Alps and never ending snow clad mountains, You will also enjoy stunning views of the Aletsch Glacier which is regarded as the longest glacier in the Alps. Get a spectacular view of the Ice Palace and the amazing view from the Sphinx Observation Terrace facing the Aletsch Glacier. Post places to visit in Interlaken or things to do in Interlaken or places to see in Interlaken. Return back to Interlaken city for overnight stay at hotel.

Day 03:- Interlaken to Lucerne and Lucerne city tour
Today after a juicy breakfast, Check out and go for a train journey from Interlaken to Lucerne, on the way enjoy a scenic view later go for coach ride over the Albis Pass and along the beautiful shores of Lake Lucerne. Later On arrival and post hotel check-in formalities, go for a short city tour of Lucerne and start visiting the Lion Monument, Chapel Bridge and the new Concert and Congress Hall. Post places to visit in Lucerne or things to do in Lucerne or places to see in Lucerne. Return back to Lucerne city for an overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 04:- Mt Titlis day trip from Lucerne
Morning post healthy breakfast at hotel, go for full day excursion trip to Mt Titlis from Lucerne. Proceed to Mount Titlis for the most exciting tour on this Europe holiday package from India going through the world's first revolving cable car. Enjoy the breathtaking sight of the glacier's deep crevasses and gigantic ice boulders from the Rotair, the lift of the revolving gondola allows you an uninterpreted 360-degree view of the spectacular landscape. Post places to visit in Mount Titlis  or things to do in Mount Titlis  or places to see in Mount Titlis . Return back to Lucerne city for an overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 05:- Lucerne to Paris (by flight/Own Expense)
Enjoy your delicious breakfast at hotel, Check out from hotel after memorable Switzerland holiday tour with Paris tour package from Bangalore and move the Airport to board flight to Paris. On arrival at Paris meet our representative and drop to your pre-booked hotel in Paris. Rest of the day free to explore Paris city by your own. You may go for the Louvre Museum tour or visit Disneyland or go for the world renowned Lido Show in Paris. Overnight in Paris.

Day 06:- Paris Hop on Hop off City Tour or Places to visit in Paris tour
Post tempting breakfast at hotel, leave your hotel on your London and Paris tour package from Delhi for Paris Hop On Hop Off bus tour or Paris city tour by Hop On Hop Off bus go for the top attraction to visit in Paris starts by visiting sacred Notre Dame Cathedral is medieval Catholic cathedral church, It is regarded as one of the finest examples of French Gothic architecture, Its construction started in 1160 under supervision of Bishop Maurice de Sully and was mostly complete by 1260. Next stop the Avenue des Champs Elysees - It is famously defined as the world's best avenue and it is almost 2kms in length and 70 meters wide, this historic avenue running between the Place de la Concorde to the majestic Arc de Triomphe. Visit Place de la Concorde - one of the most popular public squares in Paris created in 1772, it is the largest square in the French capital. It is located at the end of the Champs-Elysees and surrounded by prestigious hotels and two monumental fountains, Later visit the Arc de Triomphe - is one of the famous monuments, Standing at the western end of the Champs-Élysées. At last visit most famous monument and engineering Marvel the Eiffel Tower which is the most visited paid monument of the world more than 7 million tourists visit Eiffel tower every year. The Iconic Eiffel Tower in Paris is a wrought-iron tower and It is named after the Gustave Eiffel engineer whose company was behind designing and building of the tower from 1887 to 1889. You may rise-up to its 2nd floor to enjoy a breathtaking view of the surroundings of Paris. In the evening you may go for Seine River Cruises in Paris to witness the beauty of Paris with evening lighting. Overnight in Paris.

Day 07:- Full Day Disneyland Park tour in Paris (own expense)
Breakfast at hotel, Day is free or you can choose an optional tour to Euro Disney. Disneyland Park and Disney Studios - 1 Day 2 Parks - By coach This is a one day pass to Disneyland Resort Paris that gives a full day access to both Disneyland Paris and Walt Disney Studios Parks. This includes transfer and return on board a luxury motor coach from the centre of Paris to Disneyland Paris and admission 'hopper ticket' for Walt Disney Studios Park and the Disneyland Park. You can first decide to step through the Walt Disney Studios Gates, let your imagination soar and immerse yourself in the never-ending, always fascinating world of movies and television. Then, you can access Disneyland Park where an enchanting kingdom filled with fun, fantasy and discovery will form before your eyes, a world of make-believe characters, shows and attractions. By visiting both Parks, going from one to the other as many times as you wish, you will discover the most magical place on earth inspired by the most magical dream of all yours

Day 08:- Paris Departure
This morning post tasty breakfast at hotel, Check out from hotel on London and Paris tour package from Hyderabad and drop to Paris airport on seat in coach (SIC). return home with lot of memorable memories of your London-Paris Tour Package from Delhi, Mumbai, Chennai, Hyderabad and Bangalore
