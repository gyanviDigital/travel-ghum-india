Itinerary At Glance:

Our 21 Day, Tigers and Temples tour of India is an ultimate safari through four of the top National and tiger reserve Parks in India. This tour gives a chance to explore real culture and tradition of the small cities, when combined with our wildlife tour, This is the perfect way to explore real India in depth for both culture and wildlife. Along with a city tour of historical cities of Delhi,  Taj Mahal visit in Agra , Jaipur city tour and UNESCO Heritage Khajuraho temples tour. This wildlife tour packages in India give you chance to go to jungle safari in Kaziranga national park as well as Elephant safari in Kaziranga, Morning and Evening Jungle Safari in Corbett which is India 's First National Tiger Reserve forest, Also go for Ranthambore Jeep Safari on your India wildlife tour packages, bandhavgarh and kanha national parks safari on your Tigers with Indian Golden Triangle tours, Plan your memorable India wildlife tours with Ghum India Ghum as well as luxury wildlife package and tiger safari trip india with Ghum India Ghum and get lifelong experience.

Itinerary at Glance :-
Day 01:- Arrive Delhi for Central India Temple Tour
On arrival in Delhi for your Incredible India tour with wildlife tour packages in india. After completion of your immigration formalities and luggage collection come out of exit gate no 5 of Indira Gandhi International Airport, Delhi. Our friendly representative will be waiting for you with your name Placard board. Later transfer to your pre-booked hotel in a comfortable spacious car. Rest or the day free for Relaxing. Overnight in Delhi.

Day 02:- Delhi Local Sightseeing
Post Delicious breakfast at hotel, gear up for full day Delhi city tour, Start with Old Delhi city tour by visiting- The Red Fort, Jama Masjid, The silver street of Chandni Chowk, Spice market, Raj Ghat. For a better understanding of the Old Delhi tour you may go for a Rickshaw ride tour and ride through the bazaars and streets of Old Delhi (Optional tour). Later Post Lunch go for New Delhi city tour visit The famous Hindu temple -Birla Mandir, Akshardham, Kalkaji Temple, The India Gate, The Rashtrapati Bhawan - President's House, The tomb of Emperor Humayun, Qutab Minar and The Bahai Temple(Lotus Temple) if time permits than also visit Bangla sahib Gurdwara (Sikh Temple). Post completion of Place to visit in Delhi tour or best itinerary for Delhi tour, stay overnight in Delhi hotel.

Day 03:- Delhi Guwahati (by flight/ own Expense) to Kaziranga National Park (217kms / 5 hrs)
After tasty breakfast, Check out from hotel and drop to Delhi airport to board flight to Guwahati. On arrival at Guwahati airport, meet and greet then transfer to Kaziranga National park is in Assam spread across Brahmaputra river, Kaziranga National Park is famous for the world’s largest population of Indian one-horned Rhinoceroses. On the way you may visit Elephant Falls Shillong is situated in Upper Shillong and it is Elephant like stone at its foot, Gunner’s Falls 1 - situated near to the military establishment on Upper Shillong road and Shillong Peak is famous View Point Shillong, it offers stunning panoramic view of the entire Shillong city. On arrival at Kaziranga National park, Check-in to your hotel on arrival, Kaziranga national park hotels famous for its jungle themed stays. Overnight stay in Kaziranga.

Day 04:- Kaziranga national park safari and travel to Guwahati ( 217 kms / 5 hrs)
Early morning Elephant ride (Additional cost). Post yummy breakfast at hotel go for jeep safari in Kaziranga or Kaziranga Jungle safari (additional cost) to Central Range, Go for jungle Safari by open Jeep in Kaziranga with naturalist in search of world famous one-horned rhinos, elephants tigers, jackal, languor, monkeys and the different species of deer as well as many domestic and migratory bird species. Post lunch, check out and drive to next destination Guwahati capital city of Assam State. On arrival check-in to your hotel . Rest of the day feee for Relaxing. Overnight stay in Guwahati.

Day 05:- Guwahati to Delhi(by flight/own expense) Delhi to Corbett (250 kms/5-6 hrs)
This morning post tempting breakfast at hotel check out and drop to airport to board flight to Delhi. On arrival at Delhi Railway station or Airport, Meet and greet our friendly driver come representative and later drive to next adventure destination to India 's First National Tiger Reserve forest, the Corbett National Park - is the oldest national park of India and was estd in 1936 as Hailey National Park to preserve the endangered Bengal tiger. It is located in between  Nainital and Pauri Garhwal district of Uttarakhand and it was named after Jim Corbett. On Arrival at Corbett and Check in to your resort. Rest of the day is at leisure for individual activities, staying overnight at the resort in Corbett.

Day 06:- Corbett National Park (morning and evening safari on additional cost)
This early morning proceeds for jungle safari tour or jeep safari in Corbett at 6:00 AM (additional cost). This is the most exciting day of the tour for wildlife lover safari or wildlife safari in Corbett where you will get a chance to see different animals inside  Jim Corbett national park and later back to the hotel have your healthy breakfast and rest. Rest of the day free for Leisure you may go for Corbett fall or Corbett Museum tour . Overnight stay in Corbett.

Day 07:- Corbett to Delhi (250 kms/5-6 hrs)
After mouth watering breakfast, check out from the hotel and proceed to Delhi, which is India’s capital territory, is a massive metropolitan area in the country’s north. Delhi which is a national capital and a starting point of the tour. Divided into two parts namely New Delhi and Old Delhi, endowed with a rich cultural and historical background. On arrival in Delhi check in to the hotel. Night stay in Delhi.

Day 08:- Delhi to Jaipur ( 260 kms/ Approx. 5 hrs)
After a healthy breakfast at hotel, Start your Rhino and Tigers Tour from Delhi and drive to Jaipur popularly known as pink city India. This is the only city in the world symbolising the nine divisions of the universe through the nine rectangular sectors subdividing it. The historic walled city of Jaipur in Rajasthan, which was founded in 1727 AD under the rule of Sawai Jai Singh II. Jaipur is popular for its exemplary architectural legacy and vibrant culture, it made its official entry into the UNESCO World Heritage Site list recently in 2019.  On arrival in  Jaipur in late evening & transfer to  your pre-booked  hotel. Night stay in Jaipur.

Day 09:- Jaipur Sightseeing tour
This morning post breakfast, than go for tour of UNESCO certified Jaipur city by visiting the Amber fort, built in 16th century famous for its artistic style, enjoy an elephant ride(own Expense) at Amber, later visit Jal Mahal which is situated in middle of Man Sagar Lake. Driving past the Hawa Mahal-a Palace of winds,This architectural feature allowed cool air from the Venturi effect to pass through, thus making the whole area more pleasant during the high temperatures in summer. In the afternoon post Lunch you will visit the city palace and museum the magnificent pillars, lattice or jali work, carved marble interiors and inlaid ornamentations make the palace beautiful in appearance and a treat to watch for the visitors, walk to adjacent Jantar Mantar an astronomical observatory, you may also visit famous Albert hall. Post Jaipur sightseeing trip you will be able to get answers to famous question Places to see in Jaipur. Stay overnight at a hotel in Jaipur.

Day 10- Jaipur to Ranthambhore (By road Approx. 04 Hrs)
This morning post delicious breakfast at hotel, Check out from hotel and start your 5th day of Rajasthan holiday packages by driving to Ranthambore-famous for the wildlife sanctuary which is said to have one of the India's friendliest Tigers and also considered to be the best place in the world to photograph the tiger in its natural habitat, also a wonderful place for bird watching. Arrive Ranthambhore & transfer to hotel. Night stay in Ranthambhore.

Day 11:- Visit Ranthambore National Park (morning and evening safari on additional cost)
Today is most exciting day of the tour for wildlife lover wake up at  early morning  for breathtaking  3 hrs Jungle safari or wildlife safari in Ranthambore in a Canter (a vehicle which is open on the top)  you will get chance to see different animal  inside Ranthambore  national park and back to the hotel have your healthy breakfast and take rest. In the afternoon post lunch​, may go for second visit to a jungle safari in India  or wildlife safari in India and enjoy the game viewing again. Night stay in Ranthambhore.

Day 12:- Ranthambore to Bharatpur (260kms/6kms)
Check out and Later you will be transferred by road to Bharatpur which is located on eastern part of Rajasthan, Keoladeo National Park is famous and major bird sanctuary in Bharatpur which have man-made wetlands that protect more than 360 species of birds which includes migratory and resident birds. It is also the main wintering area in India for huge numbers of aquatic birds from Turkmenistan, China, Afghanistan and Siberia most surprisingly is the rare Siberian crane, which has been recorded & sported in the park. Post hotel check in and lunch, visit Keoladeo National Park also known as Bharatpur Birds Sanctuary (optional) for spending quality bird watching in India. Overnight at the Hotel.

Day 13:- Bharatpur to Agra(55kms/1hr) and Agra sightseeing tour
Post delicious breakfast at your hotel, start 7th  day of Golden Triangle India with Bharatpur tour by driving to Mughal city Agra and on arrival check into your hotel. This afternoon visit the Agra fort or Red fort of Agra, built by the great Emperor Akbar, this was main residence of Mughal rulers till 1638 buit of Red stand stones and post lunch visit world famous Taj Mahal-the symbol of love, built by Emperor Shah Jahan which was commissioned in 1632 to house the remains of his beloved wife Mumtaz Mahal. You may also visit the Tomb of I'timād-ud-Daulah also known as Baby Taj Mahal . By evening return to your pre-booked hotel for overnight stay.​ Note:- Taj Mahal closed on Friday

Day 14:- Agra to Jhansi (By train) Jhansi - Orchha -Khajuraho
After breakfast transfer to railway station to board the fast air-conditioned train to Jhansi. After arriving at Jhansi railway station, meet ​and greet our friendly representative and drive to Khajuraho on the way to visit Orchha. Orchha is an architectural heritage town in Niwari district of Madhya Pradesh, Orchha historical town was founded by Rudra Pratap Singh in 16th century from Bundela dynasty. Later Bir Singh Deo, king of Orchha who was a great builder during his time, spread Bundeli architectural styles not only in Orchha but to several parts of North India. On arrival post hotel check-in formalities, go for a place to see in Orchha. Orchha is the well- preserved medieval township, Orchha was built in the 16th century. Orchha Sightseeing include tour of Rajmahal with its beautiful murals, also visit the Laxmi Narayan Temple and the Jehangir Mahal built in the 17th century, to commemorate the visit of the emperor Jehangir.Post tour of Places to visit in Orchha or Things to do in Orchha, Post tour continue your drive to Khajuraho for overnight stay in your pre-booked hotel.

Day 15:- Khajuraho City Tour
Post yummy breakfast at hotel go for places to see in Khajuraho or Khajuraho guided tour start with visiting Khajuraho and its temples, Explore Khajuraho Eastern and Western Group of Temple, these temples built during the the Chandela dynasty rule from 950-1050. Out of 85 temples only 22 temples from the original temples group survived till now, All these temples are magnificent examples of Hindu architecture. Though all the temples are worth a visit, you visit the main attractions of Khajuraho which are the Parasvanath Temple, the Adinath temple. Post tour of places to visit in Khajuraho or best itinerary for Khajuraho tour or things to do in Khajuraho​, go for The Khajuraho Group of Monuments tour which is of Hindu temples and Jain temples, these temples UNESCO World Heritage Site since 1986. Stay overnight in Khajuraho.

Other exciting additional tours for this day on direct payment post tourist places to visit in Khajuraho you may opt for Evening Panna Jungle safari tour from Khajuraho, Panna is only 1hr drive from Khajuraho. Panna national park is the 22nd National park of India and it was declared in 1992. Evening Jungle Safari in Panna will let you experience the dense ambience of forest and give you a chance to encounter the wildest ranges of mammals, reptiles, several species of migratory birds, flora and fauna. Other than this, you will even be able to get the chance to spot the tiger species at very close quarters, Later you will return back to the hotel for Night stay in Khajuraho.


Day 16:- Khajuraho to Bandhavgarh (230kms/5-6hrs)
This morning post delicious breakfast at hotel, Check out and drive to Bandhavgarh most famous destination in India for tiger safari on your Golden Triangle With Tigers. Bandhavgarh is a place of legendary and mythological Importance. The famous historical Bandhavgarh Fort is of immense significance as it has been gifted by Lord Rama to his younger brother Lakshmana.'Bandhav' means brother and ‘Garh’ stand for Fort. The history of Bandhavgarh goes dates back to more than 2000 years ago and due to this you find many man made caves with rock paintings and inscriptions.On arrival check into to pre-booked jungle resort in Bandhavgarh, be ready for next day as per Wildlife safari timing in Bandhavgarh. Rest of the day is free for relaxing.

Day 17:-Jeep Safari in Bandhavgarh National Park (morning and evening safari at additional cost)
Bandhavgarh National Park is located in Central state Madhya Pradesh India. This park is popular for maximum density of Indian royal Bengal tigers, especially in its central Tala zone. Other animals include leopards, deer and white tigers. Earlier it was the hunting ground of rulers of Rewa. Today's Morning and Evening go for 02 Jeep Safari in Bandhavgarh or tiger safari in Bandhavgarh which is part of your package by our Naturalist.During Bandhavgarh safari package , you will get chance to see various type of animal species such as Sambar deer, Panther, Wild Boar, Spotted Deer, Gaur, Nilgai (Blue bull), etc. Another exciting thing is, you will be able to spot around 280 species of birds, many of them migratory one, that will leave you with an unforgettable lifelong bird watching experience. Today​ after Breakfast, Lunch and Dinner at the hotel. Night stay in Bandhavgarh

Day 18:- Bandhavgarh to Kanha National park (220kms/4-5 hrs)
This morning post yummy breakfast at jungle resort in Bandhavgarh, Check out and drive to the next destination on your tiger safari tours  in india is Kanha Tiger Reserve. It is situated in the madla/ balaghat districts of Madhya Pradesh, India. This park is located almost in 940 sq.km, Kanha National Park is one of the largest and finest parks in Madhya Pradesh where the sensational royal Bengal tigers are found in good numbers. Kanha Tiger Reserve is honored all across the globe for saving the Barasingha from almost extinction. It is considered one of the best managed parks in Asia and a part of Project Tiger. National Geographic's award winning documentary 'Land of the Tigers' was shot here, but the park is also home to leopards, wild dogs and Barasingha as well. On arrival at Kanha Jungle Lodge after a long journey check in and rest of time free for relaxing. Overnight at a resort in Kanha.

Day 19:- Kanha national park safari (morning and evening safari on additional cost)
Kanha National park is considered as the land of the tiger, here you will find more tigers. Kanha Jungle Safari gives you the best opportunity ​to escape from your daily routine. It's a famous saying that A safari in Kanha will surely get you an unforgettable thrill, Just the way you were on your first trip to the zoo, as a child. On your Morning and Evening Safari in Kanha Tiger reserve You may spot world famous Royal Bengal Tiger and other species of animals like the sloth bear, leopards, Indian wild dog, Barasingha and around 300 avifauna species. In addition, you will also get a chance to witness the beautiful Sunrise and sunset view at the Bamni Dadar point that will add on to the overall charm of your wildlife tour in India. Kanha national park is known for its evergreen Sal forests, Apart from Tigers it is home to almost 300 species of Birds and many of them are migratory one, 26 species of Reptiles, 43 species of Mammals and more than 500 species of Insects. Today ​after Breakfast, Lunch and Dinner at the hotel. Night stay in Jungle lodge in Kanha.  

Day 20:- Kanha National park to Jabalpur Airport (170kms/3-4hrs) and Jabalpur to Delhi by flight (Own Expense)
This morning after tempting breakfast at hotel with forest in background, Check out from hotel to drop to Nearest airport to Kanha national park is Jabalpur airport which is well connected to Delhi, Mumbai, Hyderabad, Bhopal and other major cities or Drop to The nearest railway stations from Kanha tiger reserve  is Bilaspur. On arrival in Delhi our representative picks you up and drops you to a pre booked hotel in Delhi. Rest of the Day free for relaxing and exploring delhi by your own. Night stay in Delhi.

Day 21:- Delhi Dropping
This is the last day of your tour and post mouth watering breakfast, pack your bag and baggage and check from the hotel. Return home with a lot of memorable memories of your golden triangle with tiger safari tour or Golden Triangle with Wildlife tours in India or Indian Wildlife and Golden Triangle Tour Package or India's Golden Triangle and look for tigers,  drop to Delhi airport as per your flight timings.
