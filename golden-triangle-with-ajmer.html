Overview

Embark on an enriching 6-day journey through the heart of Northern India with our Golden Triangle tour with Ajmer. This meticulously crafted tour takes you via the traditionally huge towns of Delhi, Agra, Jaipur, and the holy metropolis of Ajmer within the culturally rich country of Rajasthan. The tour now not only promises a glimpse into the colorful tapestry of Northern India but also offers a religious retreat inside the sacred ambiance of Ajmer.


Delve into the beauty of Delhi's harmonious blend of modernity and Mughal-era history,setting the stage for an unforgettable adventure. Traverse to Agra,where the iconic Taj Mahal stands as a testomony to undying love, after which to Jaipur, revealing regal hill forts and palaces that narrate the royal history of Rajasthan. Ajmer, the holy city, provides spiritual contact with the revered Ajmer Sharif Dargah, and the 16th-century palaces cover the Ajmer Authority Museum.

 

Discover the finest of Northern India with us—we provide prompt confirmation, convenient pick-up and drop-off options, knowledgeable tour guides, and a very engaging cultural encounter. To find out the wonders of this appealing area, reserve your 5-night or 6-day Golden Triangle tour with Ajmer now. A once-in-a-lifetime revel awaits you.

Itinerary:

Download Itinerary
01
Day 01: Arrive in Delhi for the Incredible Delhi Tour

Things-To-Do: Old Delhi Exploration, Enjoy Savory Street Food, Cycle Rickshaw Ride, Visit to Museums, visit to Gurudwara Bangla Sahib, Shopping

 

On arrival at Delhi airport, our representative will warmly welcome you at the exit gate of Delhi-Airport for the Golden Triangle Tour with Ajmer. After the greetings start your incredible journey by visiting Laxmi Narayana Mandir, India Gate, Rastrapati Bhawan. Later, move for an antique Delhi city visit—through journeying the Raj Ghat, riding hostoric red fortress, then visiting Jama Masjid, India's largest mosque—are crucial places to go. You can also visit Chandni Chowk, the spice market, etc. For more information on the antique Delhi tour, you could go on a Rickshaw experience tour and ride via the bazaars and streets of vintage Delhi (non-obligatory price). After a enjoyable lunch, continue a tour of New Delhi’s wonders. The Qutub Minar, Lotus Temple and other places if time permits. Head back to your accommodation. A fantastic night's stay awaits for you.

Note: The Red Fort and Lotus Temple are closed on Mondays.

 

02
Day 02:- Drive to Agra - visit the Taj Mahal and Agra Fort

Things-To-Do: Tonga Ride, Mehtab Bagh, Mughal Heritage Walk, Savor Famous Sweets of Agra (Don’t Miss Agra Ka Petha), visit to Marbel Factory

 

Begin the second day of the Golden Triangle tour with Ajmer by way of riding to Mughal city Agra (220 km/4 hours), and on arrival, take a look at your hotel. This afternoon, go to the Agra fort, built by the Great Emperor Akbar. After lunch, go to the globally famous and one of the most famed wonders of the world- The Taj Mahal, the symbol of love, constructed by Emperor Shah Jahan for his beloved wife, Mumtaz. You may also visit the Tomb of I'timād-ud-Daulah, also known as the Baby Taj Mahal. After finishing your Agra sightseeing or designing the ideal Agra trip itinerary, spend the night in an Agra hotel.

Note: Taj Mahal closed on Friday

 

03
Day 03:- Drive to Jaipur

Fatepur Sikri -Things-To-Do: Jodha Bai Palace, Panch Mahal, Buland Darwaja, Diwan-i-Khas and Diwan-i-Aam


Embark on the fourth day of your Golden Triangle Tour with Ajmer after a healthy breakfast. Head to Jaipur, renowned for its pink metropolis. Along the way, discover Fatehpur Sikri, a nicely preserved red sandstone ghost city that served as the capital of Mughal ruler Akbar. Maintain your journey to Jaipur, founded in 1727 by Sawai Jai Singh II. This ancient walled city stands out for its flat terrain, contrary to the hilly landscapes of its counterparts. Take a look right into a pre-booked inn upon arrival for an evening's stay in Jaipur.

04
Day 04:-Have a look at Jaipuir Highlights

Things-To-Do: Elephant Ride & Camel Ride, Hot Air Balloon Ride, Chokhi Dhani,Jaipur Literature Festival (if timing aligns)

 

After breakfast on the fourth day of the Golden Triangle Tour with Ajmer, visit the magnificent Amber Fort to begin your exploration of Jaipur; finish it early to beat the crowds. Explore its remarkable development and historical treasures such as Sheesh Mahal. Continue to the fascinating Jal Mahal, a palace situated in the heart of Sagar Lake. See the stunning architecture of Hawa Mahal, which was created to suit the discerning tastes of the royal family. After Lunch, explore the city palace, and Jantar Mantar's. Visit the Instagram-worthy Patrika Gate and Albert Hall to round off your day before heading back to the resort for an overnight stay in Jaipur.

 

05
Day 05:- Drive to Ajmer & visit Ajmer

Things-To-Do: Dine at Ajmer Restaurants, visit Local Bazaars, Pushkar Camel Fair, Daulat Bagh, explore Jagat Palace (if time permits)

 

Embark on a charming adventure from Jaipur to Ajmer. Following a satisfying breakfast, the day unfolds with a visit to the respected Ajmer Hazrat Khwaja Garib Nawaz Dargah. Dive into the wealthy records of Ajmer, a gem in Rajasthan, as you explore the enduring Dargah Sharif and Shah Jahan's mosque. Wander through the southern part of Ajmer to find out about the enchanting man-made Ana Sagar Lake. Have a look at a mystical charisma intensified by the soul-stirring qawwali performances. Conclude the day's exploration and unwind at your Jaipur hotel, a retreat from the bustling Islamic Pilgrimage India tour.

06
Day 06:- Head Towards Delhi For Your Departure

After a hearty breakfast, as your journey comes to an end, pack your memories of the Incredible Golden Triangle Tour with Ajmer Journey. After you check out of your Delhi hotel, the driver's representative will make sure you get to the airport on time, in accordance with the time of your return flight. Think back on your time in India and its fascinating culture while you travel. Return home with a lifetime of priceless memories. Write a review and offer your thoughts on the Golden Triangle Tour with Ajmer 6 Days with Ghum India before you depart.
